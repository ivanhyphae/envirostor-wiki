The Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) and the Water Board (WB) have reviewed the revised Soil Management Plan (SMP) dated February 8, 2021 which describes the excavation, confirmation sampling, handling, and proper disposal of impacted soil for the two proposed retention basins associated with new construction along State Route 132 (SR 132).
