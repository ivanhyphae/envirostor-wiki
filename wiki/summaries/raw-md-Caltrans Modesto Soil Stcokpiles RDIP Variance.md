## Document identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Variance to a Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP Variance) — addresses a change in the source of clean cover fill material for capping barium-containing soil (BCS) in Stockpiles 1 and 2 on the SR 132 West Project.
- **Author or preparer:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. (John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG).
- **Date or period covered:** April 9, 2020; references data from sampling in early 2020.

## Key claims
- The original RDIP (Jan 2019) required DTSC pre-approval of clean fill sources.
- The initially approved source (Carpenter Road) is insufficient; the contractor identified a second potential source: Mechanically Stabilized Earth (MSE) wall footing excavations along the southern boundaries of Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- For Stockpile 1, the upper foot of the MSE wall footing excavation contains elevated barium and is unsuitable for clean cover. Soil from 1 to 5 feet depth contains barium concentrations (42 to 140 mg/kg) with a 95% UCL of 113.1 mg/kg, within the site-specific naturally occurring background range (17 to 120 mg/kg), making it suitable for use as clean cover fill.
- Background soil samples from boring SR99/132-BG08 show barium concentrations ranging 50–110 mg/kg, with a calculated soil background value (UCL 95 / arithmetic mean) of 72.8 mg/kg, well below OEHHA screening values. Other metals (antimony, arsenic, beryllium, cadmium, chromium, cobalt, copper, lead, mercury, molybdenum, nickel, selenium, silver, thallium, vanadium, zinc) were also reported with concentrations mostly non-detect or low.
- The Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing material is yet to be evaluated.

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** Approval of a variance to a Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP).
- **Agency:** California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) — requested the variance; co-regulator is Central Valley RWQCB.
- **Conditions:** DTSC must pre-approve the Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing material after it is sampled and analyzed for barium and lead against site-specific background concentrations.

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants:** Barium (primary), Lead, and other heavy metals (antimony, arsenic, beryllium, cadmium, chromium, cobalt, copper, mercury, molybdenum, nickel, selenium, silver, thallium, vanadium, zinc).
- **Media:** Soil (MSE wall footing excavation, Stockpile 1; background soil from boring SR99/132-BG08).
- **Concentration ranges (Stockpile 1, depth 1-5 ft):**
    - Total Barium: 42 – 140 mg/kg
    - Total Lead: <1.0 – 6.6 mg/kg
- **ProUCL 95% Student's-t UCL (Barium):** 113.1 mg/kg.
- **Background soil barium concentration:** 50–110 mg/kg. UCL 95 / arithmetic mean: 72.8 mg/kg.
- **Applicable thresholds:**
    - Site-specific max background barium: 120 mg/kg (background range: 17–120 mg/kg).
    - BCS Removal Verification Threshold: 1,000 mg/kg.
    - Max site-specific background lead: 3.8 mg/kg.
    - OEHHA 2005 Residential screening value: 5,200 mg/kg; Commercial/Industrial: 63,000 mg/kg.
- **Exceedances noted:** The surface sample in boring STK1-3 (STK1-3-0 at 0 ft) had 420 mg/kg barium and 6.7 mg/kg lead, exceeding background and upper-foot limits for clean fill.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Geotechnical evaluation of MSE wall footing excavation material as a source of clean cover fill for capping BCS in Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Phase status:** Completed for Stockpile 1; pending for Stockpile 2 (after southern slope BCS material is excavated).
- **Quantities:** 5-foot-deep excavation along the southern boundary of each stockpile.
- **Verification outcomes:** The 1–5 ft interval in Stockpile 1 is deemed suitable as clean cover fill.

## Responsible parties
- **California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC):** Regulatory oversight and pre-approval of clean fill sources.
- **Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB):** Co-regulator.
- **Caltrans:** Owner/operator.
- **Geocon Consultants, Inc.:** Prepared the variance and performed the evaluation.
- **Bay Cities Paving and Grading:** Highway contractor that identified the fill sources.

## Key terms and conditions
- **Stockpile 2 MSE Wall Footing Evaluation:** Must be completed after southern slope BCS excavation; samples must be analyzed for barium and lead and compared to site-specific background to determine suitability.
- **Other conditions:** Only two clean fill sources are proposed: the approved Carpenter Road borrow source and any DTSC-approved wall footing material.
