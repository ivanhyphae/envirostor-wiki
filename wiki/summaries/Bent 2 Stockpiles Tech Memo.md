## Key claims
- The Bent 2 excavation soil stockpiles do not meet the clean cap criteria for barium established in the DTSC-approved variance document (upperbound background threshold of 120 mg/kg for barium).
- Barium was detected in all 10 samples at concentrations ranging from 31 to 230 mg/kg; 4 of 10 samples had barium concentrations at or above 120 mg/kg.
- Lead was detected in 6 of 10 samples at concentrations ranging from 2.6 to 13 mg/kg, within the range of the maximum site-specific background of 3.8 mg/kg.
- The material may be suitable for use as partial clean cap (6 inches or less) on the upper surface of Stockpile 2 if approved by DTSC, because additional clean cap (structural pavement section) will be placed over it.
- Fill from previously approved clean fill sources will be required for placement of clean cap on the northerly slope of Stockpile 2.

## Methodology
- **Experimental setup:** Ten discrete soil samples (STK1–STK10) were collected from the Bent 2 end dump piles, placed in two rows extending approximately 800 feet along the southern portion of Stockpile 2. Samples were spaced at approximately 80-foot intervals, alternating between end dump rows.
- **Sample handling:** Samples were placed in labelled Ziploc plastic bags and transported to California Laboratory Services (CLS) under chain-of-custody protocol.
- **Analytical methods:** Each soil sample was analyzed for barium and lead using EPA Test Method 6010B under expedited 24-hour turnaround.
- **Evaluation metrics:** Results were compared to the maximum site-specific background values (120 mg/kg for barium, 3.8 mg/kg for lead) and to the BCS Removal Verification Threshold (1,000 mg/kg for barium, 80 mg/kg for lead).

## Results
- **Barium** was detected in all 10 samples. Concentrations (mg/kg): STK1=230, STK2=120, STK3=170, STK4=47, STK5=49, STK6=220, STK7=54, STK8=50, STK9=31, STK10=48. Range: 31–230 mg/kg. Four samples (STK1, STK2, STK3, STK6) had concentrations ≥120 mg/kg (maximum site-specific background). All samples were below the BCS Removal Verification Threshold of 1,000 mg/kg.
- **Lead** was detected in 6 of 10 samples. Concentrations (mg/kg): STK1=13, STK2=7.6, STK3=5.9, STK6=4.2, STK8=2.9, STK10=2.6; STK4, STK5, STK7, STK9 were below the reporting limit of 2.5 mg/kg. Range: 2.6–13 mg/kg. The maximum detected lead (13 mg/kg) exceeded the maximum site-specific background of 3.8 mg/kg but was well below the BCS Removal Verification Threshold of 80 mg/kg.

## Limitations
- The results are based on a single round of sampling (10 samples) from the Bent 2 end dump piles; spatial variability across the stockpile may not be fully captured.
- The determination of suitability for partial clean cap is pending DTSC review and approval; the memo does not constitute a final regulatory decision.
- Only two analytes (barium and lead) were tested; other potential contaminants were not assessed.
- The memo does not discuss long-term stability or leachability of the contaminants.
