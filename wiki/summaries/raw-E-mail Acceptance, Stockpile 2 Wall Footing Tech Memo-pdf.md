## Regulatory decisions

- **Decision type:** Official acceptance of the Tech Memo (via email)
- **Deciding agency and date:** DTSC, June 22, 2020; also concurrence noted from RWQCB
- **Conditions or requirements imposed:**
  - Soil 0–3 ft bgs must be placed in designated containment zones.
  - Soil >3 ft bgs may be used as embankment fill or capping material.
  - Use of sub‑3‑ft material is covered by the April 9, 2020 VR to the RDIP; no additional VR required.
- **Documents approved or acted on:** “Stockpile 2 Wall Footing Tech Memo” (revised version dated June 18, 2020)
