## Key claims
This document is a **Log of Test Borings (LOTB)** for the North Carpenter Road Overcrossing (Bridge No. 2636) in Modesto, CA. It provides a detailed subsurface profile from four test borings (A-17-084, A-17-085, A-17-086, and an additional boring at Sta 19+25). Key findings include the identification of various soil layers down to approximately elevation -51.3 ft, including silty sands (SM), clayey silts (ML/CL), poorly graded sands (SP), and sandy silts (ML), with groundwater strikes (GWS) recorded on 11-09-17. The borings were terminated at depths where very dense or hard soils were encountered, with Energy Ratios (ERi) of 69% at termination.

## Methodology
Four test borings were drilled at specified locations relative to the proposed bridge alignment. Relatively undisturbed soil samples were obtained using a **3-inch OD (2.4-inch ID) California Modified split-spoon sampler** and a **2-inch OD split-spoon sampler (Standard Penetration Test)**. A **140-pound automatic hammer falling 30 inches** was used to drive the samplers. Blow counts (field blow counts per 6 or 12 inches) were recorded, and for relative density determination with the California Modified sampler, a **sampler correction factor of 0.7** was applied. Visual classification of earth materials was performed per the **Caltrans Soil & Rock Logging, Classification, and Presentation Manual (2010)** and confirmed or revised with lab tests. Test boring locations were determined by staking and pacing from mapped features. The vertical datum is NAVD 88.

## Results
The document reports blow counts (e.g., "50/6 2.4" indicating 50 blows over the last 6 inches of a 2.4-inch ID sampler) and pocket penetrometer (PP) readings (up to **4.5 tsf** in hard soils). Key soil strata encountered include:
- **0-8 ft**: Asphalt concrete (8") over aggregate base (4").
- **Varying depths**: Silty sand (SM) – medium dense to very dense; Clayey silt (ML/CL) – hard; Sandy silt (ML) – very stiff to hard (PP=3.25-4.5 tsf).
- **Deeper layers (below ~40 ft)**: Very dense poorly graded sand (SP) and hard sandy lean clay (CL) with PP=4.5 tsf.
- **Groundwater**: Strikes (GWS) were noted on 11-09-17 and 11-07-17.
- **Termination depths**: Ranged from elev. -51.3 ft (A-17-086) to elev. 2.2 ft (A-17-084), with ERi=69%.

## Limitations
- **Location accuracy**: Only to the degree implied by staking/pacing from mapped features.
- **Subsurface variability**: Soil and groundwater conditions between adjacent test holes and at other locations may differ from those shown; conditions may change over time.
- **Sampler correction**: Blow counts are field values; a correction factor (0.7) was applied for density descriptors with the California Modified sampler, implying blow counts are not directly comparable to SPT N-values without correction.
- **Interpretation**: Boring logs represent the professional opinion of the engineer/geologist based on soil character at specific locations.
