## Key claims
- The document describes a proposed clean fill source sampling plan for the Highway 132 project in Modesto, California.
- The plan targets a 5‑ft‑deep soil interval (0–5 ft) along the state right‑of‑way east of Carpenter Road, historically agricultural land since the 1960s.
- Previous environmental investigations (Blackburn 2014 ISA, Rincon 2018 Phase II ESA) found no high‑risk documented contamination releases in the immediate vicinity; residential backyard testing showed pesticides and metals below regulatory screening levels.
- Proposed sampling includes 12 borings (5‑ft depth) with six depth‑discrete composite samples analyzed for organochlorine pesticides (OCPs) and heavy metals.

## Methodology
- **Sampling design**: 12 borings, each 5 ft deep, in the clean fill source area.
- **Sample preparation**: 4‑point composite samples formed from discrete depth intervals.
  - 0–2 ft: three composite samples analyzed for OCPs and heavy metals.
  - 3–5 ft: three composite samples analyzed for heavy metals (OCPs not specified for this interval).
- **Procedures**: Field work planned under contract authorization, USA notification (mark‑out or 72‑hour notice), and pending DTSC sampling plan approval.
- **Analytical metrics**: No numerical thresholds are given; the plan references regulatory screening levels from earlier studies.
- **Logistical details**: Drilling scheduled for February 11, 2020; 48‑hour analytical turnaround if needed.

## Results
- **No quantitative results are reported**; this is a pre‑implantation sampling plan. Results from the subsequent analyses were to be provided to DTSC after completion.

## Limitations
- The plan is **site‑specific**: limited to the Carpenter Road–east Highway 132 alignment (state right‑of‑way) and to the uppermost 5 ft of soil.
- Only OCPs and heavy metals are targeted; other potential contaminants (e.g., volatile organic compounds, petroleum hydrocarbons) are not addressed.
- The low‑risk conclusion relies on previous studies that only tested residential backyards; the source area itself was not directly sampled before this plan.
- The plan is **not a completed study**—it awaits DTSC approval and field execution before any data are generated.
