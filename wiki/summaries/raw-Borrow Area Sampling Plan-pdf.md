## Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered**: Modesto Highway 132 (SR 132) alignment, Stanislaus County, California. Proposed clean fill source area extends from Carpenter Road eastward along the SR 132 right-of-way. This area has been State right-of-way since the 1960s and was agricultural property prior.
- **Media addressed**: Soil (proposed clean fill source for BCS stockpile cover)
- **Specific sample locations or work areas referenced**: 
  - 12 proposed borings, each to 5 feet depth, within the clean fill source area.
  - Test borings completed at Carpenter Road in 2017 (referenced for lithologic description; logs appended in original transmission but not included in extracted text).
