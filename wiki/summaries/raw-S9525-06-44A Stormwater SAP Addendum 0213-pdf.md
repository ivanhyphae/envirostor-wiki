## Site and Scope
- **Site name:** Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles
- **Location:** Southerly of intersection of State Route (SR) 99 and Kansas Avenue, Stanislaus County, California. Approximate boundaries and Stockpiles 1 through 3 shown on Site Plan (Figure 2).
- **Areas of concern covered:** Stockpile 1 (south of Kansas Ave, west of North Emerald Ave), Stockpile 2 (south of Kansas Ave, between North Emerald Ave and SR 99), Stockpile 3 (south of Kansas Ave, east of SR 99). Former FMC evaporation/disposal ponds (approximate boundaries shown on Figure 2).
- **Media addressed:** Stormwater / surface water runoff
- **Specific sample locations referenced:** Proposed locations include puddles along east and west shoulders of Emerald Avenue between Stockpiles 1 and 2; background sample along Emerald Avenue north or south of Stockpiles 1 and 2; additional locations if runoff observed leaving Caltrans right-of-way. Previous sampling location SW03 (northwestern side of Stockpile 3) referenced.

    - **Who is making it:** Geocon (this document)
    - **Supported by data?:** Conditional assertion; presented as a rationale for sampling at that location.

## Analytical Results
- **Previous sampling (March 2006, by Shaw):**
    - **Contaminants:** Dissolved metals, PAHs, nitrate, sulfate, sulfide.
    - **Media:** Surface water (stormwater) from constructed impoundments.
    - **Concentrations:** Barium reported at 2,000 µg/L in sample SW03 (northwestern side of Stockpile 3). Barium in remaining six samples ranged from 16 to 190 µg/L.
    - **Threshold:** California Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL) for barium is 1,000 µg/L.
    - **Exceedances:** Sample SW03 exceeded the barium MCL by a factor of 2 (2,000 µg/L > 1,000 µg/L). No other target analytes exceeded MCLs or site background levels.
    - **QA/QC flags:** None reported in this summary of the prior work.
- **Proposed sampling (this addendum):**
    - **Contaminants:** Title 22 total and dissolved metals (including strontium), nitrate as nitrogen, sulfate, sulfide, total suspended solids (TSS).
    - **Analytical methods:** EPA 6020/7470 (metals), EPA 300.0 (nitrate, sulfate), SM 4500 (sulfide), SM 2540D (TSS).
    - **Field measurements:** pH, electrical conductivity, temperature, turbidity.

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** This document does not describe any physical remediation work. It describes proposed surface water characterization sampling.
- **Status:** Planned. Sampling will occur during up to four separate rainfall events, collection of up to five site samples plus at least one background sample.

## Key Terms and Conditions
- **Sampling protocol:** Work to be performed in accordance with protocols approved by DTSC in the Final Surface Water Sampling and Analysis Plan (Shaw, January 2006).
- **Sampling criteria:** Samples to be collected during a qualifying rain event (defined by visible runoff and 72 hours of prior dry weather, consistent with Shaw 2006 SAP). Up to four separate rainfall events.
- **QA/QC requirements:** Use of disposable sampling scoops, chain-of-custody documentation. Laboratory QA/QC required: one method blank per 10 samples/batch; one duplicate per 10 samples/batch; one spiked sample per 10 samples/batch (spike at 10x detection limit or analyte level).
- **Deliverable:** A summary report will be prepared including background summary, scope, field observations, laboratory results, and data evaluation.
