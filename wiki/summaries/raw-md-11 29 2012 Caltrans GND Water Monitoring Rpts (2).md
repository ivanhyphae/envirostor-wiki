- **Document type and purpose:** DTSC concurrence and response letter regarding three Groundwater Monitoring Reports (May, June, and July 2012) for the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles project. - **Author or preparer:** Randy S. Adams, C.E.G., Senior Engineering Geologist, Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Date or period covered:** Letter dated November 29, 2012. Monitoring periods covered: May 2012, June 2012, and July 2012.

## Key claims
- **Claim:** DTSC, in consultation with the RWQCB, has reviewed the three groundwater monitoring reports and concurs with their findings.
- **Attribution:** DTSC (Randy S. Adams) and RWQCB (consulted).
- **Claim:** The reports present analytical results of groundwater monitoring well sampling and discusses the installation of two new monitoring wells (MW-9 and MW-10) in June 2012.
- **Attribution:** Based on reports prepared by Geocon Consultants (November 2012).

- **Decision type:** Concurrence (no formal approval or order). DTSC states it "concurs with the subject groundwater monitoring reports."
- **Agency:** DTSC (Brownfields and Environmental Restoration Program) in consultation with the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB).
- **Date:** November 29, 2012.
- **Conditions imposed:** "Requests that future groundwater monitoring reports be completed as groundwater data becomes available."
