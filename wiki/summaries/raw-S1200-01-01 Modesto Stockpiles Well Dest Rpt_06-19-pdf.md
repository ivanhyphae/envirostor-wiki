## Site and Scope
- **Site name / location:** Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles, Modesto, Stanislaus County, California (SR 132 corridor)
- **Media addressed:** Groundwater (monitoring wells) and soil (well backfill)
- **Monitoring wells covered:** Six wells destroyed: MW1, MW2, MW3, MW5, MW7, MW8. Four wells left in place at the Site (MW4, MW6, MW9, MW10 remain). Two destroyed wells were within future highway travel lanes; four were in areas of future drainage improvements.

## Key Claims
- **Claim:** Wells were abandoned in accordance with California Well Standards Bulletin 74-90, Monitoring Well Standards, Part III and City of Modesto permit requirements.
- **Claim made by:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. (preparer)
- **Support:** Supported by documented procedures (pressure grouting via tremie, over-drilling top 5 ft, backfilling), permit reference, and DWR Well Completion Reports.

## Regulatory Decisions
- **Permit:** City of Modesto Well Destruction Permit No. ENC2019-50174 (issued April 26, 2019) was obtained and fees paid prior to work.
- **Notification:** City of Modesto notified ≥48 hours before destruction; Underground Service Alert notified 72 hours before mobilization (Ticket Nos. W910200748, W910200753, W910200756, W910200763, W910200770, W910200773).
- **Reporting:** Well Completion Reports (DWR Form 188) submitted to the California Department of Water Resources (DWR) and Stanislaus County Department of Environmental Health (submission date June 12, 2019; WCR numbers 2019-008169 through 2019-008174).

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** Groundwater monitoring well destruction via pressure grouting.
- **Implementation dates:** April 29, 2019 (MW1, MW2, MW5) and April 30, 2019 (MW3, MW7, MW8).
- **Subcontractor:** Gregg Drilling and Testing (Caltrans-approved, California-certified).
- **Destruction procedure:**
  - Pre-destruction measurements: depth to groundwater and total well depth recorded.
  - Grouting: neat cement grout (3 gallons water per 47-lb sack), mixed in 5-gallon buckets, placed via tremie pipe to maximum well depth.
  - Pressure application: 20–25 psi for approximately 5 minutes.
  - Grout volumes: calculated minimum 6.4 to 7.4 gallons; actual volumes ranged from 13 to 20 gallons per well (15 gallons for MW1, MW2, MW5, MW7, MW8; 20 gallons for MW3).
  - Post-grouting: well boxes removed; top 5 ft of casing over-drilled with 8-inch-diameter hollow-stem auger.
  - Backfill: top 5 ft backfilled with a combination of cuttings, native material, and bagged topsoil (clean fill for MW5 and MW8).
- **Debris disposal:** Well box materials and concrete deposited in Caltrans’ onsite construction debris pile.
- **Phase status:** Completed.
