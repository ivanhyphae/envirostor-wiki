### Document Identity

- **Document type and purpose:** Email transmittal and attached sampling plan (prepared by Geocon) for characterizing native soil proposed for use as clean fill cover material at the Modesto Highway 132 (SR 132) project. The plan defines boring locations, sampling methodology, and analyte lists.
- **Author or preparer:** Email authored by Richard C. Stewart (Caltrans, Engineering Geologist). Sampling plan prepared by John Juhrend (Geocon Consultants, Inc.).
- **Date or period covered:** Email dated February 4, 2020. Boring fieldwork tentatively scheduled for February 11, 2020. The plan also references a 2017 Carpenter Road test boring log and historical reports from 2014 (Blackburn ISA) and 2018 (Rincon Phase II ESA).

### Key Claims

- **Clean fill source qualification:** The area proposed for soil excavation (upper 5 feet along SR 132 alignment, east from Carpenter Road) is asserted to be comprised of native soil, having been State right-of-way since the 1960s and previously agricultural property.
- **Low contaminant risk:** Claimed that no high-risk documented contamination releases are reported in the immediate vicinity of the proposed source area, based on review of prior reports.
- **Background data:** Testing of residential backyards adjacent and west of Carpenter Road (from the Rincon 2018 Phase II ESA) found pesticides and metals at concentrations below applicable regulatory screening levels.
- **Lithologic support:** The 2017 Carpenter Road test boring logs are provided to document that the material at that location is native soil, supporting the characterization of the broader source area.

### Regulatory Decisions

- **Not applicable in this document.** No formal regulatory decisions, approvals, or conditions are included; the document is a *request* for DTSC review and approval of a sampling plan.

### Analytical Results

- **Not applicable in this document.** No analytical results are reported; the document describes a *planned* analytical program.

### Remedial Actions

- **Planned Excavation:** The contractor plans to excavate the upper 5 feet of soil along the SR 132 alignment (from Carpenter Road eastward).
- **Planned Sampling:** 12 borings (5 feet deep) are planned in the clean fill source area.
    - Depth intervals: 0–2 feet and 3–5 feet.
    - Analytical program (depth-discrete, 4-point composites):
        - 0–2 feet: Organochlorine pesticides (OCPs) and heavy metals (3 composite samples).
        - 3–5 feet: Heavy metals (3 composite samples).
- **Phase Status:** Pre-remedial; sampling is for characterization of source material.
- **Verification:** No verification outcomes reported.
