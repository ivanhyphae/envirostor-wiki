## Key claims
- Soil from Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation down to 3 feet bgs must be placed in designated containment zones.
- Soil from depths greater than 3 feet bgs is suitable for use as embankment fill or capping material.
- A revised technical memo corrected a value for barium (120 mg/kg) rather than lead; this correction does not alter the overall determination about soil placement.
- The material below 3 feet bgs is considered part of the April 9, 2020 Variance Request (VR) to the Remedial Design Implementation Plan, so no additional VR is necessary.

## Methodology
- Review and acceptance of a Technical Memorandum (Memo) dated June 18, 2020 from Geocon.
- Sampling conducted along the mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) wall footing excavation area for Stockpile 2.
- Laboratory analysis of soil samples from different depths.
- Evaluation by California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) and Water Board.

## Results
- Soil down to 3 feet bgs: must be placed in designated containment zones.
- Soil below 3 feet bgs: suitable for embankment fill or capping material.
- Barium concentration corrected to 120 mg/kg in the revised memo, not lead.
- Agencies (DTSC and Water Board) agree with the Memo's determinations.

## Limitations
- No explicit limitations mentioned in the email correspondence.
- The determination is based on specific site conditions and sampling for Stockpile 2 only.
- Applicability to other stockpiles or locations is not addressed.
