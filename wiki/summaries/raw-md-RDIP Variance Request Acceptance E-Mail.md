# Document Summary: RDIP Variance Request Acceptance E-Mail

## Document Identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Formal e-mail acceptance of a Variance Request (VR) to the Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP) for the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation project (SR 132, Stanislaus County, CA). The DTSC provides official acceptance for use of specific soil as clean cap cover material from Stockpile 1's southern boundary; decision on Stockpile 2 is deferred pending data review.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, P.G. (Project Manager, Site Evaluation and Remediation Unit, Department of Toxic Substances Control).
- **Date or period covered:** April 17, 2020 (acceptance e-mail); referenced Variance Request dated April 9, 2020; original RDIP variance request e-mail from Caltrans dated April 13, 2020.

## Key Claims
- **Assertion by Caltrans (via Richard Stewart):** Bay Cities Contractors, if approved, would only use soil excavated for construction of the Stockpile 1 mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) wall footing as a source of clean cover or embankment fill within the stockpile footprints; it would not be used at other areas of the project or hauled off-site.
- **Finding by applicant (VR dated April 9, 2020):** The upper foot of planned MSE wall footing excavation soil along the southern boundary of Stockpile 1 is not suitable as clean fill due to elevated barium concentrations, and therefore will be placed in Stockpile 1 and Stockpile 2 permanent fill areas.
- **Finding by applicant:** Soil from 1 to 5 feet below existing grade in the Stockpile 1 southern boundary area contained barium concentrations with a calculated 95% upper confidence limit (UCL) of 113.1 mg/kg, which is within the range of site-specific naturally occurring background concentrations.
- **DTSC and Water Board agreement:** The 1–5 foot zone material (113.1 mg/kg UCL) is within naturally occurring background range and can be used as clean cap soil on the project.

## Regulatory Decisions
- **Agency:** DTSC (co-lead with Central Valley RWQCB as co-regulator; Water Board referenced).
- **Action type:** Formal acceptance of variance request (via e-mail); conditional approval for Stockpile 1 southern boundary soil.
- **Conditions imposed:**
  - Soil from 1–5 ft depth (barium 95% UCL = 113.1 mg/kg) along Stockpile 1 southern boundary is approved as clean cap soil.
  - Material from the upper 1 ft of excavation (elevated barium) must be placed in Stockpile 1 and Stockpile 2 permanent fill areas (not used as clean cover).
  - Soil along Stockpile 2 southern boundary is deferred for separate approval/rejection after data submission and review.
  - Use is restricted to within the stockpile footprints; cannot be used elsewhere on the project or hauled off-site.
- **Date:** April 17, 2020.

## Analytical Results
- **Contaminant:** Barium.
- **Media:** Soil (MSE wall footing excavation area, southern boundary of Stockpile 1).
- **Concentration range:**
  - Upper foot (0–1 ft): Not suitable as clean fill (elevated barium, no specific concentration given in this document).
  - 1–5 ft depth: 95% UCL = 113.1 mg/kg.
- **Threshold:** Site-specific naturally occurring background range (value not specified in document, but 113.1 mg/kg is stated to be within that range).
- **Exceedances:** Upper 1 foot exceeds clean fill criteria; lower zone (1–5 ft) is within background.

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** MSE wall footing excavation along Stockpile 1 and Stockpile 2 southern boundaries.
- **Phase:** Construction/remedial design implementation (variance to RDIP).
- **Verification outcome:** DTSC/Water Board acceptance of soil as clean cap for Stockpile 1 southern boundary (1–5 ft depth); Stockpile 2 southern boundary pending data submission/review.
- **Quantities/status:** Not specified; material from upper 1 ft of Stockpile 1 excavation will be placed in permanent fill areas of Stockpiles 1 and 2.

## Key Terms and Conditions
- **Use restriction:** Approved clean cap soil from Stockpile 1 southern boundary must be used only within the stockpile footprints; not elsewhere on the project, not hauled off-site.
- **Deferred decision:** Stockpile 2 southern boundary data must be submitted and reviewed; DTSC will provide separate approval/rejection.
- **Permanent fill:** Upper 1 ft (elevated barium) from Stockpile 1 excavation is to be placed in Stockpile 1 and Stockpile 2 permanent fill areas.
