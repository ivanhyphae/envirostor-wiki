## Site and Scope
- **Site name and location:** Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles, located south of the State Route (SR) 99/Kansas Avenue interchange in Modesto, Stanislaus County, California.
- **Areas of concern covered:** Stockpiles 1 and 2; specifically, the source of clean cover fill material for capping consolidated barium-containing soil (BCS) within these stockpiles.
- **Media addressed:** Soil (clean cover fill material).
- **Specific sample locations or work areas referenced:**
    - Carpenter Road borrow source area (within SR-132 right-of-way).
    - Mechanically Stabilized Earth (MSE) wall footing excavations along the southern boundaries of Stockpiles 1 and 2.
    - Sample IDs from Stockpile 1 MSE wall footing: STK1-1-0 through STK1-1-4, STK1-2-0 through STK1-2-4, STK1-3-0 through STK1-3-4.

## Key Claims
- **Claim:** The MSE wall footing soil along Stockpile 1, from 1 to 5 feet below grade, is suitable for use as clean cover fill material.
    - **Claimant:** Geocon Consultants, Inc., in coordination with Caltrans and DTSC.
    - **Support:** The claim is supported by analytical data presented in the document (Table 1). Barium concentrations in the 1–5 foot interval ranged from 42 to 140 mg/kg. A 95% upper confidence limit (UCL) of the mean was calculated as 113.1 mg/kg, which is within the range of site-specific naturally occurring background barium concentrations (17 to 120 mg/kg).
- **Claim:** The upper foot of the Stockpile 1 MSE wall footing soil is not suitable for clean cover fill due to elevated barium.
    - **Claimant:** Geocon, in coordination with Caltrans and DTSC.
    - **Support:** Statement is made in the text referencing the Geocon April 6, 2020 Technical Memorandum. Data from sample STK1-2-0 (240 mg/kg) and STK1-3-0 (420 mg/kg) from the 0–1 foot interval support this claim, as these exceed the 120 mg/kg site-specific background maximum.
- **Claim:** The Carpenter Road borrow source area contains suitable clean fill material.
    - **Claimant:** Geocon.
    - **Support:** Documentation references the Geocon February 27, 2020 Technical Memorandum and states that DTSC subsequently approved this source. No analytical data for this source is included in this document.

## Analytical Results
- **Contaminants measured:** Total Barium, Total Lead.
- **Media:** Soil (from Stockpile 1 MSE wall footing).
- **Concentration ranges with units:**
    - **Barium:** 42 to 420 mg/kg in the top 5 feet. Excluding the upper foot (samples at 0 ft depth), the range is 42 to 140 mg/kg.
    - **Lead:** <1.0 to 6.7 mg/kg.
- **Applicable thresholds:**
    - Site-specific naturally occurring background barium: 17 to 120 mg/kg (max 120 mg/kg).
    - Site-specific background lead: 3.8 mg/kg.
    - BCS Removal Verification Threshold: 1,000 mg/kg barium; 80 mg/kg lead.
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds:**
    - Samples STK1-2-0 (240 mg/kg barium) and STK1-3-0 (420 mg/kg barium) exceeded the site-specific background maximum of 120 mg/kg, confirming the top foot was unsuitable.

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** Identification and evaluation of clean cover fill material sources for capping Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Quantities:** The proposed clean cover cap is minimum 12 inches of clean fill or minimum 4 inches of AB (aggregate base) pending pavement/hardscape. The MSE wall footing excavation is described as approximately 5 feet deep.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:**
    - **Stockpile 1 MSE Wall Footing:** Material from 1–5 feet depth is suitable as clean cover (barium 42-140 mg/kg; 95% UCL 113.1 mg/kg, within background 17-120 mg/kg). The top foot (0–1 ft) is unsuitable for clean cover.
    - **Stockpile 2 MSE Wall Footing:** Evaluation is planned but not yet completed.
- **Phase status:** Planned (for Stockpile 2 evaluation) and Completed (for Stockpile 1 evaluation and Carpenter Road source approval). The RDIP Variance is the formal step to allow these sources to be used in the capping work.
