## Document Identity
- **Document type and purpose:** DTSC approval letter for the *Stockpile 2 BCS Foundation Spoil Assessment Workplan Technical Memorandum* (Tech Memo). The Tech Memo describes planned sampling and management of barium-containing soil (BCS) spoils generated from installation of the Weather Station and Overhead Sign #1 on Stockpile 2 at the State Route 132 West project.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, PG, Project Manager, Site Evaluation and Remediation Unit, Site Mitigation and Restoration Program, DTSC.
- **Date or period covered:** Letter dated March 4, 2022. The Tech Memo referenced is dated February 28, 2022.

## Regulatory Decisions
- **Decision:** DTSC concurred with the stockpiling and sampling procedures described in the Tech Memo.
- **Agency:** Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Date:** March 4, 2022.
- **Conditions imposed:**
  - If sampling results indicate concentrations of barium and lead are **below** established site-specific background, the material may be reused on-site at locations mutually agreed upon by DTSC and Caltrans.
  - If sampling results show spoils material is **above** background concentrations, the material should be profiled for disposal at the Forward Landfill (Manteca, CA) or another acceptable landfill facility.

## Analytical Results
- **Contaminants specified:** Barium and lead.
- **Media:** BCS foundation spoils from Stockpile 2.
- **Threshold:** Site-specific background concentrations for barium and lead (established earlier; not re‑defined in this letter).
- **No exceedances noted;** the letter addresses future sampling, not completed results.
