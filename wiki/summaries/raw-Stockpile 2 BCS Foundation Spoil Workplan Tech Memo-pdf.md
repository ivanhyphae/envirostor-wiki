## Key terms and conditions
- **Characterization sampling:** Collect eight discrete soil samples from stockpile on a grid pattern at various depths; composite into two 4-part composites; analyze for Title 22 metals under expedited 48-hour turnaround.
- **Soluble metals testing:** WET/TCLP may be required if total metal concentrations are elevated, to determine hazardous waste classification (California or federal RCRA).
- **Onsite reuse criteria:** Total barium and lead concentrations must not exceed site-specific background (120 mg/kg barium, 3.8 mg/kg lead) AND receive approval from Caltrans and DTSC.
- **Offsite disposal:** If reuse not approved, profile waste for acceptance at Forward Landfill or other facility. Authorized Caltrans representative to sign manifests; transporter must be licensed; transport trucks must have covered loads.
- **Follow-on deliverable:** A letter summary report describing BCS disturbance activities, locations/depths, volume removed, site plan, copies of laboratory reports and chain-of-custody, landfill manifests and tonnage receipts, and site photographs. This information will be included in the Removal Action Completion Report (RACR).
