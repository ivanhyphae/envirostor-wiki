Here is the structured summary of the source document for the case-file wiki.

## Document Identity
- **Document type and purpose:** E-mail chain conveying and approving a Clean Fill Sampling Plan. The Plan outlines sampling and analysis for soil to be excavated from beneath the Carpenter Avenue detour ("Shoofly") area for use as clean fill material on the Modesto SR 132/99 project.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, PG (DTSC Project Manager) – author of the approval. The sampling plan was prepared by Geocon Consultants, Inc. (John Juhrend, PE, CEG, CEM).
- **Date or period covered:** April 2, 2021 (plan transmitted) – April 7, 2021 (approval issued). Excavation planned for the week of April 19, 2021.

## Key Claims
- **Claim (Caltrans/Richard Stewart):** The proposed soil sampling is for "determination of clean fill material to be excavated beneath the current Carpenter Ave. detour." No use of the soil will occur until DTSC approval.
- **Assertion (Scott Frenette, WSP):** Material from "Retaining Wall No. 1110 has already been approved for use as clean cap material." The material under the Carpenter Shoofly requires separate testing and approval prior to use as clean cap material.

## Regulatory Decisions
- **Decision:** Formal approval of the "Carpenter Shoofly Area Clean Fill Sampling Plan" (revised version received April 5, 2021).
- **Agency:** California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Date of decision:** April 7, 2021.
- **Decision maker:** Dean Wright, PG (DTSC Project Manager).
- **Conditions noted:** The Plan was reviewed and approved as consistent with previous borrow area sampling efforts. No comments were provided.
- **Co-regulator review:** The Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (Waterboard) also reviewed the Plan, as indicated by Kyle Cockerham being cc'd on the approval e-mail.

## Analytical Results
- No analytical results are included in this document; it is only an approval of the sampling plan.

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity described:** Sampling and analysis of soil at the Carpenter Shoofly area to characterize material for use as clean fill.
- **Quantities:** Excavation depth of 20 feet; total fill volume of 5,000 cubic yards (cy).
- **Phase status:** Pre-approval / planning. No soil use was to occur until DTSC approval was granted.
- **Planned schedule:** Excavation approximately the week of April 19, 2021 (per Bay Cities Paving & Grading).

## Responsible Parties
- **Owner/Operator:** California Department of Transportation (Caltrans) – represented by Richard C. Stewart, PG (Engineering Geologist).
- **Lead Regulatory Agency:** DTSC – represented by Dean Wright, PG.
- **Co-Regulatory Agency:** Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB) – represented by Kyle Cockerham.
- **Consultant/Plan Preparer:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. – represented by John Juhrend, PE, CEG, CEM.
- **Construction Manager/General Contractor (WSP/Bay Cities Paving & Grading):**
    - **WSP USA:** Scott Frenette (Construction Manager), Rick Deml (Resident Engineer).
    - **Bay Cities Paving & Grading (BCPG):** Adan Corrales, Pablo Bernardino (contractor performing excavation).

## Key Terms and Conditions
- **Condition of Approval:** The use of soil from the Shoofly area is contingent upon DTSC approval of the sampling plan (*this e-mail serves as that approval*).
- **Basis for Approval:** The Plan was deemed "consistent with previous borrow area sampling efforts."
- **Follow-on Action:** Sampling must be completed per the approved Plan. No further comments or conditions were imposed by DTSC or the Waterboard in this correspondence.
- **Pre-existing Condition:** Material from Retaining Wall No. 1110 was previously approved as clean cap material.
