- **Document type:** Draft Interim Removal Action Completion Report (RACR)

## Site and Scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered:** Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles, along the eastern portion of the SR 132 West Project alignment, Modesto, Stanislaus County, California. Specifically addresses BCS Stockpiles 1, 2, and 3.
- **Media addressed:** Soil (primarily barium-containing soil [BCS] and aerially deposited lead [ADL]-impacted soil), groundwater, stormwater, air.
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced:**
    - **Monitoring wells:** MW-1 through MW-10. MW-1, MW-2, MW-3, MW-5, MW-7, and MW-8 were destroyed in April 2019. MW-4, MW-6, MW-9, and MW-10 remain and were sampled.
    - **BCS Stockpile work areas:** Stockpile 1 (approx. 34,000 yd³), Stockpile 2 (approx. 102,000 yd³), Stockpile 3 (approx. 24,000 yd³, fully removed).
    - **Verification sample locations:** Stockpile 3 (S3-1 through S3-26, plus S3 SE BERM and S3 SW BERM); Stockpile 2 west end (S2W-1 through S2W-8); Stockpile 2 east end (S2E-1 through S2E-12); MSE wall footing locations (STK1-1 through STK1-3; STK2-1 through STK2-7).
    - **Clean fill source areas:** SR 132 ROW adjacent to Carpenter Road, Basin 5, Bent 2 stockpiles, Carpenter Road shoofly.

## Key Claims
- **Claim:** The consolidation and clean fill capping of BCS Stockpiles 1-3 (and ADL soil) within the SR 132 highway embankment fill during Interim Phase 1 is substantially complete, with remaining work (pavement, drainage, barrier) to be finished by September 2022.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. (preparer), on behalf of Caltrans and the City of Modesto.
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported by data and descriptions of completed work.
- **Claim:** Verification soil samples from BCS removal areas (Stockpiles 2, 3) met the RDIP verification thresholds for barium (1,000 mg/kg) and lead (80 mg/kg) after additional excavation in areas with initial exceedances.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon Consultants, Inc.
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported by analytical data in Table 1 and associated Technical Memoranda in Appendices B and C.
- **Claim:** The Stockpile 1 MSE wall footing material, while containing elevated barium relative to background, is suitable for placement within the BCS Containment Zones.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon Consultants, Inc.
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported by analytical data (Table 1, Appendix C) showing barium up to 420 mg/kg (below the 1,000 mg/kg removal threshold but above the 120 mg/kg site-specific background).
- **Claim:** Air monitoring during BCS grading activities confirmed that airborne levels of total dust, barium, and lead were below designated project action and risk levels.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon Consultants, Inc.
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported by reference to the Air Monitoring Technical Memo (Appendix F). No specific numerical air monitoring data are presented in the main body.
- **Claim:** Groundwater/stormwater monitoring results were consistent with historical data and below regulatory thresholds.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon (citing Stantec reports).
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported by reference to Stantec reports (2021) and ongoing regulatory webpages. No specific numerical data presented in this document.
- **Claim:** The BCS consolidation scheme meets the intent of the RDIP by using a 4-inch Class 3 aggregate subbase as an interim cap before the final pavement section.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. (in RDIP Variance #2, September 2, 2020).
    - **Supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** Supported; DTSC-approved Variance is provided in Appendix A.

## Analytical Results
- **Contaminants measured:** Barium, Lead
- **Media:** Soil
- **Applicable thresholds:**
    - **RDIP BCS Removal Verification Thresholds:** Barium = 1,000 mg/kg (groundwater protection), Lead = 80 mg/kg (residential screening level).
    - **Site-Specific Upperbound Background Concentrations (from April 9, 2020 RDIP Variance):** Barium = 120 mg/kg, Lead = 3.8 mg/kg.
    - **RDIP clean fill material criteria:** General conformance with DTSC's Information Advisory Clean Imported Fill Material (DTSC, 2001).
- **Concentration ranges with units (from Table 1 and text):**
    - **Stockpile 3 (final verification samples, base of excavation approx. 82.5-84 ft MSL):** Barium ranged from 46 to 970 mg/kg. Lead ranged from 1.6 to 40 mg/kg. Initial exceedances (barium: 2,200; 5,100; 7,000 mg/kg) required deeper excavation.
    - **Stockpile 2 (West End, final verification samples):** Barium ranged from 54 to 760 mg/kg. Lead ranged from 1.5 to 35 mg/kg. Initial exceedances (1,100; 3,200 mg/kg barium) required deeper excavation.
    - **Stockpile 2 (East End, final verification samples):** Barium ranged from 73 to 760 mg/kg. Lead ranged from 1.6 to 35 mg/kg. Initial exceedances (1,400; 4,100 mg/kg barium) required deeper excavation.
    - **MSE Wall Footing Soil (Stockpile 1):** Barium: 42 to 420 mg/kg (mean 128.3 mg/kg, 95% UCL 171.9 mg/kg). Lead: <1.0 to 6.7 mg/kg (below 80 mg/kg threshold).
    - **MSE Wall Footing Soil (Stockpile 2):** Barium: 53 to 930 mg/kg. Lead: 3.5 to 22 mg/kg.
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much:**
    - Stockpile 3: S3-9 (7,000 mg/kg Ba), S3-10 (5,100 mg/kg Ba), S3-11 (2,200 mg/kg Ba). Areas re-excavated.
    - Stockpile 2 West End: S2W-1 (3,200 mg/kg Ba), S2W-3 (1,100 mg/kg Ba). Areas re-excavated.
    - Stockpile 2 East End: S2E-3 (1,400 mg/kg Ba), S2E-8 (4,100 mg/kg Ba). Areas re-excavated.
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations:** None explicitly stated in the main text. Methods cited: EPA 6010B for soil; NIOSH 0500 and 7300 modified for air. The document notes that storm drain backfill (S3-16) was sampled, not native soil.

## Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** Excavation, consolidation, and clean fill capping of BCS and ADL-contaminated soil.
- **Quantities:**
    - **BCS Stockpile 3 Removal:** ~27,675 yd³ (3,075 truckloads at 9 yd³/truck).
    - **BCS Stockpile 2 ends:** Removed for bridge abutment construction.
    - **ADL Soil Placement:** Removed from SR 99 northbound shoulder.
    - **Clean Fill Cap:** Min. 12-inch clean fill and/or 4-inch aggregate subbase (Class 3) plus structural pavement section (0.85 ft concrete, 0.25 ft HMA, 0.70 ft aggregate subbase).
- **Verification sampling outcomes:**
    - BCS Stockpile 3 removal area met barium and lead verification thresholds after re-excavation of hot spots.
    - BCS Stockpile 2 removal areas met barium and lead verification thresholds after re-excavation.
    - MSE wall footing material was placed in the BCS Containment Zones due to elevated barium.
- **Phase status:** In-progress. Interim Phase 1 containment is substantially complete. Remaining work includes final pavement, erosion control, and landscaping. The Final RACR is pending completion of these items and a Final RACR will be submitted after Ultimate Phase 2 (estimated 2028).
