## Site and scope

Three stockpiles present: Stockpile 1 (south of Kansas Avenue, west of North Emerald Avenue), Stockpile 2 (south of Kansas Avenue, between North Emerald Avenue and SR 99), Stockpile 3 (south of Kansas Avenue, east of SR 99). Also references former FMC facility evaporation pond area, drainage basin adjacent to Stockpile 3, and inlets on Loletta Avenue and North Emerald Avenue.

- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced:** PL1 (west side North Emerald Ave, between Stockpiles 1 & 2), PL2 (east side North Emerald Ave, between Stockpiles 1 & 2), PL3 (Bennett Street/alley behind Loletta Street, outside Caltrans right-of-way, south of Stockpile 2), PL4 (drainage basin outfall adjacent to Stockpile 3, northeast of SR 99 off-ramp to Kansas Ave), PL5 (within 3 feet of gate valve in drainage basin south of Stockpile 3), BG1 (drop inlet on Loletta Avenue, south of Stockpile 2), BG2 (drop inlet on North Emerald Avenue, north of Stockpiles 1 & 2).

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges with units:**
    - **Dissolved Metals (Table 2, January 6, 2016 samples):** Analyzed by EPA 6020/7470A. Results for PL1-PL5: Barium (31-100 µg/L), Calcium (4,500-8,500 µg/L as Ca), Potassium (1600-5100 µg/L as K), Magnesium (640-2500 µg/L as Mg), Sodium (940-2900 µg/L as Na), Strontium (56-120 µg/L), Chromium (0.52-1.2 µg/L), Copper (3.4-6.7 µg/L), Nickel (1.2-1.8 µg/L), Zinc (15-100 µg/L). Antimony was detected in PL1 (0.73 µg/L), PL4 (0.65 µg/L), PL5 (0.88 µg/L), and BG2 (2.2 µg/L). Lead detected in PL3 (1.7 µg/L). Manganese detected in PL3-PL5 (11-27 µg/L). Many metals (arsenic, beryllium, cadmium, cobalt, mercury, selenium, silver, thallium) were not detected at or above PQLs.
    - **Background samples (BG1, BG2):** Barium (25-30 µg/L), Calcium (3,800-5,300 µg/L), Chromium (0.73-14 µg/L), Copper (3.4-15 µg/L), Manganese (13-15 µg/L), Molybdenum (0.50-6.0 µg/L), Nickel (1.2-3.4 µg/L), Sodium (760-2,500 µg/L), Strontium (36-42 µg/L), Vanadium (1.6 µg/L), Zinc (10-54 µg/L).
    - **General Minerals (Table 3, January 6, 2016 samples):** Analyzed by EPA 300.0, SM methods. PL1-PL5: Chloride (0.82-2.6 mg/L), Nitrate as N (ND-0.47 mg/L), Sulfate (1.0-2.8 mg/L), Sulfide (ND-0.061 mg/L), TDS (29-63 mg/L), TSS (49-180 mg/L). Alkalinity (total as CaCO3) 16-29 mg/L. BG1/BG2: Chloride (0.74-2.5 mg/L), Nitrate as N (0.12-0.40 mg/L), Sulfate (ND-5.6 mg/L), Sulfide (0.020-0.023 mg/L), TDS (15-38 mg/L), TSS (240-430 mg/L).
- **Applicable thresholds:** Primary and Secondary Maximum Contaminant Levels (MCLs) for drinking water as per California SWRCB (September 23, 2015). Specific MCLs cited: Arsenic (10 µg/L), Barium (1,000 µg/L), Cadmium (5 µg/L), Chromium (50 µg/L), Lead (50 µg/L), Mercury (2 µg/L), Selenium (50 µg/L), Silver (100 µg/L), Nitrate as N (10 mg/L), Sulfate (250 mg/L, secondary), Chloride (250 mg/L, secondary), TDS (500 mg/L, secondary).
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much:** **None.** All reported concentrations were below their respective primary or secondary MCLs.
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations affecting interpretation:** Samples PL1-PL5 and BG2 required dilution due to high concentration of target analytes (calcium, barium, potassium, strontium, zinc). Matrix spike recoveries for total sulfide and calcium were outside acceptance limits due to possible matrix interference. The laboratory notes that the analytical batch was validated by the laboratory control sample.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Maintenance of vegetative/fiber cover and straw wattles on the western slope of Stockpile 2 to intercept and redirect surface water flow. Construction/regrading of an interceptor berm at the base of the access ramp on the south side of Stockpile 2. Placement of a gravel layer on the access ramp to prevent soil erosion.
- **Quantities:** None specified for maintenance activities. Berm regrading and gravel cover were "recently" completed per Caltrans.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** Not applicable (maintenance activity, not a verification sampling event).
- **Phase status:** Maintenance activities are ongoing. Berm construction was completed in February 2014; regrading and gravel cover were recently completed. Additional surface water sampling events are planned for the 2016 rain season.
