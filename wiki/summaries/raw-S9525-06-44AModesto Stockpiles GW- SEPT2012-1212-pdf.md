## Site and Scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern**: Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles, southerly of SR 99 and Kansas Avenue, Stanislaus County, California. Three stockpiles (1, 2, 3) of barium-contaminated fill derived from former FMC evaporation ponds.
- **Media addressed**: Groundwater.
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced**: Monitoring wells MW‑1 through MW‑10 (MW‑9 and MW‑10 installed June 2012). Stockpile areas 1, 2, 3.

## Key Claims (all from Geocon, supported by data)
- With the exception of manganese in MW‑3 and MW‑9, none of the reported dissolved metals concentrations exceeded their respective numeric water quality threshold values.
- Barium, lead, and strontium concentrations remained similar to historical levels and significantly less than thresholds.
- Nitrate as nitrogen exceeded the primary MCL of 10 mg/L in MW‑1 (12 mg/L), MW‑5 (22 mg/L), MW‑6 (18 mg/L), MW‑9 (11 mg/L), and MW‑10 (14 mg/L).
- TDS exceeded the secondary MCL of 500 mg/L in MW‑5 (590 mg/L), MW‑6 (610 mg/L), and MW‑10 (630 mg/L).
- Groundwater flow direction is toward the southeast at an average gradient of 0.0008 (September 2012).

## Analytical Results
### Contaminants Measured
- **Dissolved metals** (Title 22 plus strontium): antimony, arsenic, barium, beryllium, cadmium, chromium, cobalt, copper, lead, manganese, mercury, molybdenum, nickel, selenium, silver, thallium, vanadium, zinc; plus calcium, magnesium, potassium, sodium.
- **General minerals**: chloride, nitrate‑N, sulfate, sulfide, TDS, alkalinity (total, bicarbonate, carbonate).
- **PAHs** by EPA 8270‑SIM.

### Concentration Ranges (September 2012)
- Barium: 47 µg/L (MW‑8) to 280 µg/L (MW‑5) – threshold 1,000 µg/L (Primary MCL) / 700 µg/L (EPA HA). Barium detected in method blanks for some batches (Note B).
- Strontium: 220 µg/L (MW‑8) to 1,100 µg/L (MW‑1, MW‑5, MW‑10) – threshold 4,000 µg/L (EPA HA).
- Arsenic: 2.1 µg/L (MW‑1) to 4.7 µg/L (MW‑6) – threshold 10 µg/L (Primary MCL).
- Chromium: 1.1 µg/L (MW‑10) to 10 µg/L (MW‑6) – threshold 50 µg/L (Primary MCL).
- Vanadium: 18 µg/L (MW‑1, MW‑4, MW‑5) to 39 µg/L (MW‑6) – threshold 50 µg/L (Notification Level). Also detected in method blanks.
- Manganese: up to 74 µg/L (MW‑3) – threshold 50 µg/L (Primary MCL) – **exceeded** in MW‑3 and MW‑9.
- Nitrate‑N: 3.0 mg/L (MW‑3) to 22 mg/L (MW‑5) – threshold 10 mg/L (Primary MCL) – **exceeded** in MW‑1, MW‑5, MW‑6, MW‑9, MW‑10.
- TDS: 280 mg/L (MW‑8) to 630 mg/L (MW‑10) – threshold 500 mg/L (Secondary MCL) – **exceeded** in MW‑5, MW‑6, MW‑10.
- PAHs: All non‑detect at PQL of 0.20 µg/L.

### QA/QC Flags and Data Quality Limitations
- Equipment blank showed no detections.
- **Laboratory flags** (from ATL/ATL Global certificates):
  - **Note B**: Barium and other metals detected in associated method blanks for some batches.
  - **Note M1/M2**: Matrix spike recoveries outside acceptance limits for several metals; batch validated by laboratory control sample (LCS); potential matrix interference.
  - **Note D6**: Dilution required due to high concentrations of target analytes (applied to major cations and some metals in MW‑5, MW‑8, MW‑10).
  - **Note E**: Result above quantitation range for some analytes in matrix spike samples.
  - **Note R**: RPD value outside acceptance criteria.
- Data deemed sufficient for evaluation; no qualification needed per Geocon.
