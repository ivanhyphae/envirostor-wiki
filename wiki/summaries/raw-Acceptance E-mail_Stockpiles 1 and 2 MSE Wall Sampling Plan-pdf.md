## Analytical results
No analytical results are presented. The document references screening levels proposed in the Sampling Plan for comparison with future data:
- Barium: Maximum site‑specific background = 120 mg/kg; RDIP BCS removal threshold (groundwater protection) = 1,000 mg/kg; Construction worker ESL = 3,000 mg/kg; Residential ESL = 15,000 mg/kg; Commercial ESL = 220,000 mg/kg
- Lead: Maximum site‑specific background = 3.8 mg/kg; DTSC residential = 80 mg/kg

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Sampling of MSE footing soils for characterization and DTSC classification for on‑site reuse. Perimeter air/dust sampling (RDIP monitoring) was also being performed.
- **Quantities:** Ten sampling locations; discrete samples from 1‑foot intervals to ~4–5 feet depth at each location.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** Not yet performed
- **Phase status:** Planned/in‑progress (BCS excavation in progress, sampling imminent after removal)
