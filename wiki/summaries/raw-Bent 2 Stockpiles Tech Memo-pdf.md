- **Document type:** Technical Memorandum (characterization testing summary)
- **Preparer / author organization:** Geocon Consultants (John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG, Project Manager)
- **Recipient / addressee agency or party:** Scott Frenette, Construction Manager, WSP/City of Modesto
- **Date or reporting period covered:** October 30, 2020
- **Project number or task order:** Geocon Project No. S9108-01-01; referenced as "SR132" in lab records

## Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered:** State Route 132 Project, Modesto, California; Bent 2 excavation within SR 132 right-of-way, just west of State Route 99; soil stockpiled in two rows of end dumps on southern portion of top of Stockpile 2; a portion placed on top of a fill berm generated from lower Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation (approved as clean cap by DTSC).
- **Media addressed:** Soil (stockpiled native soil from Bent 2 excavation)
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced:** Ten discrete soil samples collected from Bent 2 end dump piles (STK1 through STK10), spaced at approximate 80-foot intervals east to west, alternating between end dump rows.

## Key claims
- **Claim:** The Bent 2 soil stockpiles do not meet the clean cap criteria as established in the referenced variance document approved by DTSC (upperbound background threshold for barium of 120 mg/kg).
  - **Who is making it:** Geocon (author) in the technical memorandum, subject to DTSC review.
  - **Supported by data in this document?** Yes – analytical results show barium in 4 of 10 samples at ≥120 mg/kg (max 230 mg/kg).
- **Claim:** The material may be suitable for use as partial clean cap (6 inches or less) on the upper surface of Stockpile 2 since additional clean cap (structural pavement section – base material and asphalt/concrete) will be placed over the entire upper surface.
  - **Who is making it:** Geocon, pending DTSC review and approval consideration.
  - **Conditional:** Yes, contingent on DTSC approval.
- **Claim:** Fill material from previously approved clean fill sources will be required for placement of clean cap on the northerly slope of Stockpile 2.
  - **Who is making it:** Geocon.
  - **Supported?** Stated as future requirement; no data in this document.

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** The document references and seeks to rely on a prior agency action: the *Variance to Remedial Design Implementation Plan, April 9, 2020*, approved by DTSC, which established clean cap criteria (upperbound background threshold for barium of 120 mg/kg). No new formal agency decision is contained in this memo; the memo reports data and makes a conditional recommendation pending DTSC review and approval.

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges:**
  - **Barium** in soil (10 samples): 31 – 230 mg/kg
  - **Lead** in soil (6 detects): 2.6 – 13 mg/kg; non-detects (<2.5 mg/kg) in 4 samples
- **Applicable thresholds:**
  - Maximum site-specific background barium: 120 mg/kg
  - Maximum site-specific background lead: 3.8 mg/kg
  - BCS Removal Verification Threshold (barium): 1,000 mg/kg
  - BCS Removal Verification Threshold (lead): 80 mg/kg
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much:**
  - Barium exceedances of background (120 mg/kg):
    - STK1: 230 mg/kg (exceeds by 110 mg/kg)
    - STK2: 120 mg/kg (equals background)
    - STK3: 170 mg/kg (exceeds by 50 mg/kg)
    - STK6: 220 mg/kg (exceeds by 100 mg/kg)
  - Lead exceedances of background (3.8 mg/kg):
    - STK1: 13 mg/kg (exceeds by 9.2 mg/kg)
    - STK2: 7.6 mg/kg (exceeds by 3.8 mg/kg)
    - STK3: 5.9 mg/kg (exceeds by 2.1 mg/kg)
    - STK6: 4.2 mg/kg (exceeds by 0.4 mg/kg)
    - STK8: 2.9 mg/kg (below background)
    - STK10: 2.6 mg/kg (below background)
  - No results exceeded BCS Removal Verification Thresholds.
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations:** None noted in the memo (lab report shows LCS and matrix spike recoveries within 75-125% limits, RPD ≤30%); all results reported by CLS under ELAP #1233 with EPA Method 6010B.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Excavation of native soil from Bent 2 (of the overcrossing structure under construction); soil placed in two rows of end dumps along southern portion of top of Stockpile 2.
- **Quantities:** Approximately 4,000 cubic yards excavated; end dumps extending approximately 800 feet.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** 10 discrete samples collected and analyzed; barium concentrations in 4 samples ≥ site-specific background; material does not meet clean cap criteria.
- **Phase status:** Characterization completed; results reported pending DTSC review; material proposed for partial clean cap use; information to be included in Removal Action Completion Report (RACR) to be submitted following completion of Stockpile 1 and 2 clean fill capping activities.
