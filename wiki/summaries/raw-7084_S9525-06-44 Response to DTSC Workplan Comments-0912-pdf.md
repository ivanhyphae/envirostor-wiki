## Key terms and conditions
- **Required follow‑on deliverables:** The Final Supplemental Site Investigation Workplan dated September 18, 2012, was submitted concurrently with this response. Separate reports are required after the investigation:
  1. Summary Report limited to findings of the Supplemental Site Investigation
  2. Updated Site Characterization Summary Report (combining new and previous characterization data)
  3. Updated Human Health Risk Assessment (incorporating new and previous data)
- **Laboratory analysis requirements:** EPA Method 6010B and 7471 apply to all boring categories. Soluble metals analysis must include both method detection limit (MDL) and practical quantitation limit (PQL); all concentrations between MDL and PQL must be reported with a “J‑flag” as an estimate.
- **Decontamination water management:** All rinse water from equipment decontamination must be contained, sampled, analyzed for metals, and disposed of appropriately.
- **Sampling intervals specified:**
  - Fenceline borings: continuous core to 5 ft; samples at 0–6 inches and every 1‑ft interval to 5 ft.
  - Perimeter borings: continuous core to 3 ft; same interval scheme.
  - Cadmium borings: samples at 0–6 inches and at approximate 5‑ft intervals (e.g., 4–5 ft, 9–10 ft, 14–15 ft, 19–20 ft).
- **Future analysis:** Additional laboratory analysis may be performed after initial results are reviewed by DTSC and RWQCB, as directed.
