### Key Claims and Findings
1. **Stormwater retained on site**: No runoff observed migrating offsite; stormwater stayed within site boundaries.
2. **No MCL exceedances**: No primary or secondary MCLs exceeded; manganese in PL3 equaled the secondary MCL (50 µg/L).
3. **Interceptor berm effective**: Berm at Stockpile 2 directed runoff within site boundaries.
4. **Historical barium (2006) isolated**: 2,000 µg/L barium at SW03 was confined to Caltrans right‑of‑way; current data consistent.
