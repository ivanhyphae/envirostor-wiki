# Document Summary: DTSC E-mail Approval of Carpenter Shoofly Clean Fill Technical Memo

## Document Identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Regulatory approval e-mail from DTSC Project Manager Dean Wright, accepting a technical memorandum (Memo) and clean fill sampling plan (Plan) for the Carpenter Shoofly area. The document also forwards a separate Plan approval from April 5, 2021.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, P.G. (DTSC); forwarded by Richard C. Stewart (Caltrans); technical memo prepared by Geocon Consultants (John Juhrend). The underlying April 5 Plan approval was also authored by Dean Wright.
- **Date or period covered:** E-mail dated April 22, 2021; reference to Memo dated April 12, 2021; reference to Plan received April 5, 2021.

## Key Claims
- **Assertion:** DTSC and the Water Board (Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board, CV-RWQCB) agree that "the composite soil sample results are consistent with naturally occurring background concentrations and meet the Barium Containing Soil clean cap criteria" (Memo, April 12, 2021).
- **Assertion:** The revised Carpenter Shoofly Area Clean Fill Sampling Plan (received April 5, 2021) is "consistent with previous borrow area sampling efforts" and that DTSC and the Water Board have no comments.
- **Attribution:** Claims made by Dean Wright, DTSC Project Manager, with concurrence from Kyle Cockerham (Water Boards).

## Regulatory Decisions
- **Decision type:** Formal acceptance/approval of a technical memorandum (Memo) and a sampling plan (Plan).
- **Agency:** California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC), with Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (CV-RWQCB) concurrence.
- **Date:** April 22, 2021 (Memo approval); April 5, 2021 (Plan approval).
- **Conditions imposed:** None explicitly stated; approval is based on sample results meeting the "Barium Containing Soil clean cap criteria" and consistency with background concentrations.
