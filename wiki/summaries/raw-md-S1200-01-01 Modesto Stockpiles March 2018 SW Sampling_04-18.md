## Combined Summary: Surface Water Sampling Report, Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles (Event of March 22, 2018)

**Document Identity:** This report documents the March 22, 2018 surface water sampling event at the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles site. Prepared by Geocon Consultants, Inc. (report dated April 24, 2018), it is part of Caltrans Contract No. 06A2184 and was submitted to the Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) and the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (CVRWQCB). Electronic data was submitted to GeoTracker.

**Key Findings and Claims:**
- Sampling followed DTSC-approved protocols (Surface Water SAP, Jan 2006; Addendum, Feb 2013) and targeted Title 22 dissolved metals and general minerals.
- The primary exceedance of a primary drinking water Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL) was **lead detected at 46 µg/L in sample PL3**, exceeding the MCL of 15 µg/L. This sample was collected from outside the Caltrans right-of-way.
- Multiple samples (PL2-PL4 and BG1) showed **manganese concentrations (54–370 µg/L) exceeding the secondary MCL of 50 µg/L**.
- No other dissolved metals or general minerals exceeded their respective MCLs. Results were generally consistent with historical data since 2013.
- No stormwater runoff was observed migrating beyond the Caltrans right-of-way. An interceptor berm at Stockpile 2 was functioning as designed.

**Analytical Results (Detailed):**
- **Samples Collected:** PL1-PL4 and BG1. PL5 and BG2 were not sampled due to insufficient water.
- **Contaminants Exceeding MCLs:** Lead (46 µg/L at PL3) and Manganese.
- **Contaminants Detected but Below MCLs:** Barium (up to 770 µg/L at PL3, below the 1000 µg/L MCL), Arsenic, Antimony, Calcium, Chromium, Cobalt, Copper, Magnesium, Molybdenum, Nickel, Potassium, Sodium, Strontium, Vanadium, Zinc, and others.
- **Not Detected:** Beryllium, Cadmium, Mercury, Selenium, Silver, Thallium.
- **Quality Control (QC):** While most QC data met acceptance criteria, the laboratory report flagged high or low matrix spike recoveries for several analytes (potassium, sodium, vanadium, calcium, copper, magnesium) in batch B8C0732. The laboratory control sample was reported as valid for these analytes.

**Regulatory and Remedial Actions:**
- No formal regulatory decisions or remedial actions were documented in this report. The sampling event was for monitoring purposes.
- Existing site management includes a vegetative/fiber cover and straw wattles on Stockpile 2, and an interceptor berm on the south side access ramp.
- Caltrans planned additional surface water sampling during qualifying rain events in 2018.

**Responsible Parties:**
- **Caltrans:** Owner/operator of the site.
- **DTSC (Dean Wright, Randy Adams):** Lead regulatory oversight.
- **CVRWQCB (Steve Meeks):** Co-regulator.
- **Geocon Consultants, Inc. (Rebecca L. Silva, John E. Juhrend):** Report preparers and field samplers.
