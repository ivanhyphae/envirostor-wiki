## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges with units:**
    - **Barium (Ba) in soil:** Final excavation samples ranged from 54 mg/kg to 760 mg/kg.
    - **Lead (Pb) in soil:** Final excavation samples ranged from 1.5 mg/kg to 35 mg/kg.
- **Applicable thresholds:**
    - Barium (groundwater protection): 1,000 mg/kg
    - Lead (residential screening level): 80 mg/kg
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much (initial samples):**
    - S2W 1-0: Barium at 3,200 mg/kg. (Exceeded 1,000 mg/kg threshold by 2,200 mg/kg)
    - S2W 3-0: Barium at 1,100 mg/kg. (Exceeded 1,000 mg/kg threshold by 100 mg/kg)
    - S2E 3-0: Barium at 1,400 mg/kg. (Exceeded 1,000 mg/kg threshold by 400 mg/kg)
    - S2E 8-0: Barium at 4,100 mg/kg. (Exceeded 1,000 mg/kg threshold by 3,100 mg/kg)
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations affecting interpretation:**
    - A matrix spike (MS) analysis for a barium sample (2000506-01) showed a recovery of 93.8%, which was outside the acceptance limits of 24-123%, though the laboratory control sample (LCS) was within limits. This was flagged with note "M2" (matrix interference).
    - A matrix spike (MS) analysis for a barium sample (2000505-01) showed a recovery of 89.7%, which was outside the acceptance limits of 24-123%, though the LCS was within limits. This was flagged with note "M2".
    - Sample S2E 3-0 required dilution (noted as "D6") due to high concentration of the target analyte. Barium PQL was 2.0 mg/kg.
    - All reported results are considered valid as the LCS for each batch met acceptance criteria.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Barium-containing soil (BCS) removal (excavation).
- **Quantities:**
    - **West End:** Initially excavated to approx. elevation 82 ft MSL. After initial samples showed elevated barium, the western half was deepened to approx. elevation 81 ft MSL.
    - **East End:** Initially excavated in the eastern half to approx. elevation 83 ft MSL. After initial samples showed elevated barium, the western half was excavated to approx. elevation 82 ft MSL. A subsequent hotspot at location S2E 8-0 was excavated to approx. elevation 81 ft MSL.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** All final verification soil samples (including those from deepened areas) contained barium and lead below the verification thresholds.
- **Phase status:** Completed. The document states that the BCS removal has met the verification thresholds and is suitable for placement of clean embankment fill.
