## Document identity
- **Document type**: Regulatory acceptance letter (email)
- **Preparer / author organization**: Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC)
- **Recipient / addressee agency or party**: Richard C. Stewart (Caltrans); cc: Perry Myers (DTSC), Steven Meeks (Central Valley RWQCB/Water Board), and others at Caltrans and DTSC
- **Date**: November 6, 2020
- **Project number or task order**: SR 132

## Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered**: SR 132 highway project, Stanislaus County, CA — specifically Bent 2 excavation soils and Stockpile 2 at the Modesto Soil Stockpiles site
- **Media addressed**: Soil
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced**: Bent 2 column excavation; Stockpile 2

## Key claims
- **Claim**: Soil sample results from the Bent 2 excavation do not meet the clean cap criteria established in the April 9, 2020 Variance Request approved by DTSC.
  - **Who is making it**: Caltrans (Richard C. Stewart) via the October 30, 2020 Technical Memorandum
  - **Whether supported**: Supported by analytical results cited in the Tech Memo; not contested in this document
- **Claim**: The Bent 2 soil may be used as a 6-inch or less cap on Stockpile 2, provided it is covered with clean base material and the asphalt/concrete roadway.
  - **Who is making it**: DTSC and Water Board (co-regulators)
  - **Whether supported**: Conditional — based on discussions subsequent to the Tech Memo submittal; establishes alternative use despite failing clean cap criteria

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type**: Conditional acceptance
- **Deciding agency and date**: DTSC (Dean Wright), November 6, 2020; concurrence from Central Valley RWQCB (Water Board)
- **Conditions or requirements imposed**:
  1. Bent 2 soil must be placed on top of Stockpile 2 at a thickness of 6 inches or less.
  2. Bent 2 soil must be covered with clean base material and the asphalt/concrete used to construct the roadway.
  3. Rainy season surface water sampling must be conducted as outlined in the Revised SWPPP Amendment dated October 22, 2020.
  4. Erosion control measures from the Revised SWPPP Amendment must be followed strictly.
- **Documents approved or acted on**:
  - Technical Memorandum (Tech Memo) dated October 30, 2020 (characterization of excavated Bent 2 soils for use as clean cap material) — accepted
  - Revised Stormwater Prevention Plan (SWPPP) Amendment dated October 22, 2020 — accepted

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges with units**: Not specified numerically in this email; soil sample results from Bent 2 excavation exceeded background criteria established for clean cap cover material (per the April 9, 2020 Variance Request)
- **Applicable thresholds**: Clean cap criteria established in the April 9, 2020 Variance Request; background criteria for use of soil as clean cap cover material
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much**: Bent 2 column excavation soils exceeded background criteria; specific exceedances not quantified in this communication
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations**: Not discussed

## Remedial actions
- **Activity**: Placement of excavated Bent 2 soil as a cap (≤6 inches) over Stockpile 2, covered with clean base material and asphalt/concrete roadway
- **Quantities**: Not specified
- **Verification sampling outcomes**: Not discussed; the Tech Memo results indicated soils failed clean cap criteria
- **Phase status**: Planned — acknowledged that Bent 2 soils may be temporarily exposed during winter months due to project schedule

## Responsible parties
- **Caltrans (Department of Transportation)**: Owner/operator; submitted Tech Memo; responsible for implementing capping, surface water sampling, and erosion control (via Richard C. Stewart, Engineering Geologist)
- **DTSC**: Co-regulator; issued acceptance and conditions (Dean Wright, Project Manager)
- **Central Valley RWQCB (Water Board)**: Co-regulator; concurred with decision (Steven Meeks)

## Key terms and conditions
- Bent 2 soil placement on Stockpile 2 limited to ≤6 inches thickness
- Clean base material and asphalt/concrete roadway cover required over Bent 2 soil
- Rainy season surface water sampling must be conducted per the Revised SWPPP Amendment (October 22, 2020)
- Erosion control measures from the Revised SWPPP Amendment must be strictly followed during winter months while soils are temporarily exposed
