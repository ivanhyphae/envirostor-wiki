## Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered**: Stockpile 3, State Route 132 right-of-way, Modesto, California. Area located adjacent to and east of State Route 99 between Kansas Avenue and the Modesto Irrigation District (MID) water canal.
- **Media addressed**: Soil (barium-containing soil, BCS)
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced**: 24 initial verification sample locations (S3 1-0 through S3 24-0), plus two berm samples (S3 SE BERM, S3 SW BERM) and three resample locations (S3 9A-0, S3 10A-0, S3 11A-0). Excavation area Stockpile 3.

## Key claims
- **Claim**: Stockpile 3 BCS removal has met the barium and lead verification thresholds and is suitable for placement of clean embankment fill and other highway improvements without further BCS RDIP monitoring requirements.
  - **Who is making it**: Geocon Consultants, Inc. (preparer/consultant)
  - **Supported by data**: Yes. All final excavation verification soil samples contained barium and lead below the RDIP verification thresholds. Barium concentrations ranged from 46 to 970 mg/kg; lead concentrations from 1.6 to 40 mg/kg.
- **Claim**: Areas with initial elevated barium concentrations (S3 9-0 through S3 11-0) were deepened approximately one foot and resampled, with resample results below thresholds.
  - **Who is making it**: Geocon Consultants, Inc.
  - **Supported by data**: Yes. Resample results: S3 9A-0 (barium 160 mg/kg, lead 2.2 mg/kg), S3 10A-0 (barium 160 mg/kg, lead 3.7 mg/kg), S3 11A-0 (barium 220 mg/kg, lead 2.3 mg/kg) — all below thresholds.

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges with units**:
  - Barium (total, soil): Range 46–970 mg/kg in final samples (excluding initial failed samples at S3 9-0, 10-0, 11-0 before resampling)
  - Lead (total, soil): Range 1.6–40 mg/kg in all final samples
- **Applicable thresholds**:
  - Barium: 1,000 mg/kg (groundwater protection)
  - Lead: 80 mg/kg (residential screening level)
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much**:
  - Initial samples exceeding barium threshold (1,000 mg/kg):
    - S3 9-0: 7,000 mg/kg (7× threshold)
    - S3 10-0: 5,100 mg/kg (5.1× threshold)
    - S3 11-0: 2,200 mg/kg (2.2× threshold)
  - All resample results (S3 9A-0, S3 10A-0, S3 11A-0) were below thresholds.
  - No lead results exceeded the 80 mg/kg threshold; maximum was 40 mg/kg (S3 4-0).
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations affecting interpretation**:
  - Matrix spike recovery outside acceptance limits for barium and lead in several batches (B0B0533, B0C0089, B0C0287) due to possible matrix interference, validated by laboratory control sample (M2 flag).
  - S3 9-0, S3 10-0, and S3 11-0 analyzed at 10× dilution for barium due to high concentrations.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity**: Excavation of barium-containing soil (BCS) from Stockpile 3 and transport for placement as embankment fill within the designated Stockpile 1 fill containment zone.
- **Quantities**: 3,075 truckloads, approximately 38,440 cubic yards (preliminary estimate based on 12.5 cubic yards per truck).
- **Excavation depths**: Excavated to approximate elevations ranging between 82.5 and 84 feet above MSL (original ground surface elevation 84 feet above MSL).
- **Verification sampling**: 24 initial verification samples collected between February 28 and March 12, 2020 from the base of the excavation. Three areas deepened ~1 foot and resampled (S3 9A-0, S3 10A-0, S3 11A-0). Two additional berm samples collected (S3 SE BERM, S3 SW BERM). Total of 29 samples analyzed.
- **Sampling method**: New stainless-steel sample tubes, transported to Advanced Technology Laboratories (ATL) under chain of custody protocol.
- **Analytical method**: EPA Method 6010B (ICP-AES) for total barium and lead.
- **Phase status**: Completed. The memo concludes removal has met verification thresholds and site is suitable for clean embankment fill and highway improvements.
