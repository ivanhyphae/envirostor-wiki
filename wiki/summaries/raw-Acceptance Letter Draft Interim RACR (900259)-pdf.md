- **Document type:** Acceptance letter (regulatory correspondence).

- **Project number:** Site Code 900259.

## Site and scope
- **Site name/location:** Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles, State Route 132 West Project, Modesto, Stanislaus County, California. The site consists of Barium-Containing Soil (BCS) Stockpiles 1, 2, and 3 within the Caltrans SR 132 right-of-way.
- **Areas of concern covered:**
    - Stockpile 1: ~600 ft long (E-W), 160 ft wide, estimated 34,000 yd³.
    - Stockpile 2: ~1,650 ft long (E-W), 160 ft wide, estimated 102,000 yd³.
    - Stockpile 3: ~1,100 ft long, 120 ft wide, estimated 24,000 yd³.
- **Media addressed:** Soil (barium and lead contamination). Groundwater is mentioned only in the context of future O&M Plan (monitoring obligation).
- **Specific sample locations / work areas:**
    - Excavation area of Stockpile 3 (29 confirmation samples from bottom).
    - Western and eastern ends of Stockpile 2 (8 + 12 verification sample locations).
    - Southern slopes of Stockpiles 1 and 2 (samples at 200-ft intervals, 1–5 ft depth).
    - Unpaved shoulder of SR 99 adjacent to former Stockpile 3 (aerially deposited lead).

## Key claims
- **Claim 1:** Work completed to date was accomplished as per the approved Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP) or the two variance requests (approved April 17, 2020 and September 21, 2020).
    - **Who:** Geocon Consultants (via Draft Phase 1 RACR). DTSC accepts this as stated.
    - **Support:** Supported by the document’s summary of activities and DTSC’s acceptance with no comments.
- **Claim 2:** Confirmation sampling showed that after deepening problem areas, remaining soil in the Stockpile 3 excavation was below cleanup goals for barium (1,000 mg/kg) and lead (80 mg/kg).
    - **Who:** Geocon/Caltrans (as reported in Draft RACR). DTSC does not contest.
    - **Support:** Supported by data: initial three barium exceedances deepened ~1 ft; final samples below thresholds.
- **Claim 3:** Verification sampling of western and eastern ends of Stockpile 2 showed that after deepening four exceeding areas by an additional foot, all remaining soil was below thresholds of 1,000 mg/kg (barium) and 80 mg/kg (lead).
    - **Who:** Geocon/Caltrans.
    - **Support:** Supported by reported resampling and final results.
- **Claim 4:** Soil removed from southern slopes of Stockpiles 1 and 2 had barium and lead below cleanup goals but barium above the site-specific background (120 mg/kg). Lead was within background.
    - **Who:** Geocon/Caltrans.
    - **Support:** Supported by sampling results cited in document.
- **Claim 5:** Three of four potential clean fill source areas were found acceptable per DTSC’s “Information Advisory Clean Imported Fill Material” (2001).
    - **Who:** Geocon/Caltrans.
    - **Support:** Implied by report acceptance; no specific data given.

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** Acceptance of the Draft Interim Phase 1 Removal Action Completion Report (RACR) as a technical status report with no comments.
- **Deciding agency:** DTSC (in consultation with Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board [RWQCB]).
- **Date:** February 24, 2022.
- **Conditions or requirements imposed:**
    - As-Built Plans and cross-section figures, final survey data, and Operation and Maintenance (O&M) Plan remain to be completed.
    - These components must be included in the Final Phase 1 RACR, anticipated July/August 2022.
    - The O&M Plan must include a soil management plan, annual inspection of pavement and containment features, and groundwater monitoring.
    - After Final Phase 1 RACR approval, DTSC will enter an O&M Agreement with Caltrans and complete a land use covenant for the containment areas.
    - Certification of the Remedial Action Plan (RAP) will not occur until after the Ultimate Phase 2 build-out (anticipated in 8–10 years).
    - Ultimate Phase 2 will require a supplemental RDIP for DTSC review/approval, field oversight, a Phase 2 RACR, and possible revisions to the O&M Plan and Agreement.
- **Documents approved or acted upon:** Draft Phase 1 RACR (submitted by Geocon on behalf of Caltrans). Also references approvals of two RDIP variance requests (April 17, 2020 and September 21, 2020) and the RAP (March 14, 2018).

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured:** Barium (Ba) and lead (Pb) in soil.
- **Applicable thresholds:**
    - Cleanup goals: Ba 1,000 mg/kg; Pb 80 mg/kg.
    - Site-specific background for Ba: 120 mg/kg (used to distinguish from cleanup goal).
- **Specific results:**
    - **Stockpile 3 excavation (base):** 29 confirmation samples. Initial three samples >1,000 mg/kg Ba; after deepening ~1 ft, final samples below both thresholds.
    - **Stockpile 2 ends (western 8 samples, eastern 12 samples):** Initial four areas >1,000 mg/kg Ba; after deepening 1 ft, all final samples below thresholds.
    - **Southern slopes of Stockpiles 1&2:** All samples for Ba and Pb below cleanup goals; Pb within background; Ba above 120 mg/kg background but below 1,000 mg/kg.
    - **Aerially deposited lead on SR 99 shoulder:** Removed and placed in Stockpile 1 containment zone; no numerical results provided in this letter.
- **QA/QC flags:** None mentioned in the letter; document accepts report without comment.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity type:** Excavation, consolidation, capping, slope steepening.
- **Quantities:**
    - Stockpile 3 excavated: ~27,675 yd³ transported and placed as embankment fill within Stockpile 1 containment zone.
    - Stockpile 2 ends: removed using scrapers, placed directly within Stockpile 2 containment zone.
    - Southern slopes of Stockpiles 1&2: soil removed to steepen slopes for MSE wall construction; placed as embankment fill in Stockpile 1 and 2 containment zones.
    - Aerially deposited lead soil removed from SR 99 shoulder; placed in eastern portion of Stockpile 1 containment zone.
- **Capping:** Clean fill material from three acceptable off-site sources placed over containment zones. Northern slopes vegetated with hydroseed, erosion control blanket, and fiber rolls. South side had no erosion controls due to MSE wall.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** All excavation areas met cleanup goals after deepened zones were resampled.
- **Phase status:** Interim Phase 1 construction completed; Final Phase 1 RACR pending (including As-Built plans, survey data, O&M Plan). Ultimate Phase 2 planned in 8–10 years.
