### Document Summary: Final RDIP Approval Letter (900259)

### Document identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Final approval letter for the Remedial Design Implementation Plan – Interim Phase 1 (RDIP-Interim Phase 1). This letter formally communicates DTSC’s approval of the plan, which governs the removal and re-deposition of a portion of the Modesto Soil Stockpiles to support construction of State Route 132.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, PG, Project Manager, Site Mitigation and Restoration Program, Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Date or period covered:** April 3, 2019 (letter date). The RDIP-Interim Phase 1 document is dated January 30, 2019. The associated “City of Modesto Book 2 of 3 100% Special Provisions” document was revised on March 28, 2019.

### Key claims
- **Assertions:** The approved RDIP-Interim Phase 1 and the associated 100% Special Provisions document are sufficient to ensure human health and the environment are protected during and after the Interim Phase 1 construction project.

### Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** Final Approval.
- **Agency:** Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC), in consultation with the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB).
- **Date:** April 3, 2019.
- **Conditions imposed:** No additional comments on the RDIP-Interim Phase 1 or the revised 100% Special Provisions document. The approval is conditional upon the contractor(s) being subject to the requirements found in the RDIP-Interim Phase 1, as stipulated in the approved non-standard specifications document.

### Analytical results
- **No analytical data provided in this document.** The document is an approval letter and does not contain concentration data, thresholds, or exceedances.

### Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Removal and re-deposition of a portion of the Modesto Soil Stockpiles.
- **Phase status:** Interim Phase 1.
- **Verification outcomes:** The RDIP-Interim Phase 1 describes the safety measures and sampling to be completed. The 100% design plans are included as excerpts.

### Responsible parties
- **Owner/Operator:** California Department of Transportation (Caltrans).
- **Preparer/Consultant:** Geocon Consultants, Inc. (Geocon). (Submitted the plan on behalf of Caltrans).
- **Regulator (Lead):** Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Regulator (Co-regulator):** Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB).
- **Recipient (Contractor/Consultant PM):** Mr. John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG, Geocon Consultants, Inc.

### Key terms and conditions
- **Required follow-on actions:** The contractor(s) selected for the project must be subject to the requirements found in the approved RDIP-Interim Phase 1.
- **Monitoring obligations:** The RDIP-Interim Phase 1 details the safety measures and sampling that will be completed to ensure protection of human health and the environment.
- **Restrictions:** The document serves as the final approval for the Interim Phase 1 construction project.
