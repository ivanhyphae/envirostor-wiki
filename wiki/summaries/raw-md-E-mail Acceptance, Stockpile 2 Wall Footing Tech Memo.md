## Document identity
- **Document type and purpose:** E-mail correspondence serving as DTSC’s official acceptance of a revised Geocon technical memorandum (June 18, 2020) regarding sampling results and soil disposition recommendations for the Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation area.
- **Author or preparer:** Dean Wright, P.G. (DTSC Project Manager) – acceptance e‑mail; Richard C. Stewart (Caltrans) – transmitted the memo; Geocon – prepared the memo.
- **Date or period covered:** Acceptance e‑mail dated June 22, 2020; referenced memo dated June 18, 2020.

## Key claims
- Soil from Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation to a depth of 3 feet below ground surface (bgs) must be placed in designated containment zones (barium‑contaminated fill).
- Soil from depths greater than 3 feet bgs is suitable for use in containment zones, embankment fill, or capping material.
- The soil below 3 feet bgs along the MSE wall footing is considered part of the April 9, 2020 Variance Request to the Remedial Design Implementation Plan; therefore, a new variance request is not required before using that soil.

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** Agency acceptance (via e‑mail) of technical memorandum conclusions and recommendations.
- **Agency:** DTSC (Dean Wright) with concurrence of the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (Stevens Meeks).
- **Date:** June 22, 2020
- **Conditions imposed:** Soil down to 3 ft bgs must go to containment zones; deeper soil may be used as embankment fill/capping material consistent with existing April 9, 2020 Variance Request.

## Analytical results
- **Contaminant:** Barium (originally referenced as 120 mg/kg value – corrected from lead).
- **Media:** Soil from Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation.
- **Concentration ranges:** Not explicitly stated in the e‑mail beyond the 120 mg/kg value for barium.
- **Applicable thresholds / exceedances:** Not specified in this correspondence; the memo’s determination about the 3‑ft threshold is based on those analytical results.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Sampling along the Stockpile 2 mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) wall footing excavation area; characterization and segregation of excavated soil.
- **Quantities / phase:** Not quantified.
- **Verification outcomes:** Geo‑chemical data support separation at 3 ft bgs; upper layer (0–3 ft) requires containment, deeper soil is acceptable for embankment/capping.

## Responsible parties
- **Caltrans (Richard C. Stewart, PG 7926)** – owner/operator; submitted the memo for agency review.
- **DTSC (Dean Wright, project manager; Perry Myers)** – lead regulatory oversight; issued acceptance.
- **Central Valley RWQCB (Steven Meeks)** – co‑regulator; concurred with DTSC’s acceptance.
- **Geocon (John Juhrend)** – prepared the technical memorandum.
- **WSP (Michael Scott, Ryan George)** – listed as recipients; likely environmental consultant.

## Key terms and conditions
- Soil depth threshold: **3 feet bgs** – upper layer must be placed in containment zones; deeper soil may be used as embankment fill or capping material.
- No additional Variance Request is needed because the deeper soil is already covered under the **April 9, 2020 Variance Request** to the Remedial Design Implementation Plan.
- Follow‑on actions: Implementation of the soil segregation plan consistent with the accepted memo and existing VR.
