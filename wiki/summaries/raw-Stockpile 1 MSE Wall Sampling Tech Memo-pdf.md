## Site and scope

- **Specific sample locations**: Trenches STK1‑1, STK1‑2, STK1‑3 at approximate 200‑foot intervals, each sampled at 0–4 ft depth (samples STK1‑1‑0 through STK1‑1‑4, STK1‑2‑0 through STK1‑2‑4, STK1‑3‑0 through STK1‑3‑4). Total 15 discrete soil samples to a maximum depth of 5 ft.

## Key claims
- **Claim**: Excavated material from the Stockpile 1 MSE wall footing (southerly slope) is suitable for placement within the Stockpiles 1 and 2 fill containment zones, as general bridge embankment fill, or as capping material, pending regulatory agency approval.
- **Claimant**: Geocon Consultants, Inc. (preparer/consultant)
- **Data support**: Supported by analytical results (barium 42–420 mg/kg, all < RDIP threshold of 1,000 mg/kg; lead 1.1–6.7 mg/kg, all < RDIP threshold of 80 mg/kg) and statistical summary (95% H‑UCL for barium = 171.9 mg/kg). Conditional on regulatory approval.

## Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured**: Total barium and total lead (EPA Method 6010B)
- **Media**: Soil (mg/kg dry weight)
- **Concentration ranges**:
  - Barium: 42–420 mg/kg (all 15 samples)
  - Lead: <1.0–6.7 mg/kg (14 detects; one sample ND)
- **Applicable thresholds**:
  - Site‑specific maximum background: barium 120 mg/kg, lead 3.8 mg/kg
  - RDIP removal threshold: barium 1,000 mg/kg (groundwater quality protection), lead 80 mg/kg (residential land use)
  - SF Bay RWQCB Environmental Screening Levels (ESLs) for barium: construction worker 3,000 mg/kg, residential 15,000 mg/kg, commercial 220,000 mg/kg
- **Exceedances**:
  - Barium: 9 of 15 samples exceed the background maximum of 120 mg/kg (range 130–420 mg/kg); none exceed the RDIP threshold of 1,000 mg/kg
  - Lead: 4 samples exceed background maximum of 3.8 mg/kg (range 3.9–6.7 mg/kg); none exceed the RDIP threshold of 80 mg/kg
- **Statistical summary** (barium dataset): mean = 128.3 mg/kg, 95% H‑UCL = 171.9 mg/kg (data lognormally distributed per ProUCL output). Lead statistics not calculated.
- **QA/QC**: Laboratory (ATL, ELAP 1838) reported all results; blank and LCS recoveries within limits; matrix spike recoveries for barium (110% LCS, 84.7% MS) and lead (90.7% LCS, 84.7% MS) within control limits; no data qualification flags noted.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity**: Excavation of barium‑containing soil (BCS) along the Stockpile 1 southerly slope; material transported and placed as embankment fill within the designated Stockpile 1 fill containment zone.
- **Quantities**: Not specified
- **Verification sampling**: Characterization testing performed at mid‑point of wall footing excavation in three trenches (15 discrete samples, one‑foot intervals to 5 ft depth) after removal of BCS.
- **Phase status**: Completed (excavation and placement); characterization testing results used to support reuse recommendations.
