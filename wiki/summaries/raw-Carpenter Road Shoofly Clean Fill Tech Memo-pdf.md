Here is the structured summary of the Carpenter Road Shoofly Clean Fill Tech Memo for the remediation project wiki.

### Document identity
- **Document type:** Technical Memorandum (Tech Memo)
- **Preparer / author organization:** Geocon Consultants
- **Recipient / addressee agency or party:** Rick Demi, Construction Manager, WSP/City of Modesto
- **Date or reporting period covered:** April 12, 2021 (sampling occurred April 7, 2021)
- **Project number or task order:** Geocon Project No. S9108-01-01

### Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered:** Carpenter Road Shoofly, within State Route 132 right-of-way, Modesto, California. The area is a slope face adjacent to the Carpenter Road overcrossing structure. The soil is intended as clean capping material for BCS Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Media addressed:** Soil (native soil to be excavated from the shoofly)
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced:**
    - Six vertical transects: CSF1 through CSF6.
    - Sample locations on the slope face at 2, 5, 10, and 15 feet from the top of the slope.
    - Eight 3-part composite samples: CSF1,CSF2,CSF3-2; CSF1,CSF2,CSF3-5; CSF1,CSF2,CSF3-10; CSF1,CSF2,CSF3-15; CSF4,CSF5,CSF6-2; CSF4,CSF5,CSF6-5; CSF4,CSF5,CSF6-10; CSF4,CSF5,CSF6-15.

### Key claims
- **The claim:** Soil material generated from excavation of the Carpenter Road Shoofly meets the "BCS clean cap criteria" established in the referenced variance document.
- **Who is making it:** John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG, Project Manager, Geocon Consultants.
- **Whether it is supported by data in this document, conditional, or contested:** The claim is directly supported by the analytical data presented in the document. Barium and lead concentrations in all eight composite samples are below the stated maximum site-specific background values (120 mg/kg for barium, 3.8 mg/kg for lead). The claim is not conditional or contested within the document.

### Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** The document does not contain a new regulatory decision. It references and seeks to provide data to support compliance with a prior decision.
- **Documents approved or acted on:** The memo explicitly references the "Variance to Remedial Design Implementation Plan, April 9, 2020" as approved by the California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC). This variance established the BCS clean cap criteria (upperbound background threshold for barium of 120 mg/kg).

### Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured, media, concentration ranges with units:** Soil samples were analyzed for total barium and total lead.
    - **Barium:** Detected in all 8 composites at concentrations ranging from **28 to 96 mg/kg**.
    - **Lead:** Detected in 7 of 8 composites at concentrations ranging from **1.4 to 3.8 mg/kg**. One composite (CSF1,CSF2,CSF3-15) had lead below the reporting limit (<1.3 mg/kg).
- **Applicable thresholds:** Site-specific maximum background values: **Barium = 120 mg/kg; Lead = 3.8 mg/kg**.
- **Which wells or locations exceeded thresholds and by how much:** No exceedances were reported. All detected concentrations were below the respective site-specific background thresholds.
- **QA/QC flags or data quality limitations affecting interpretation:** The laboratory (CLS) report indicates quality control was within acceptable limits (LCS recovery: Ba 107%, Pb 105%; MS recovery: Ba 107%, Pb 95%). No data quality flags were noted. The analytical method was EPA 6010B.

### Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Characterization testing of native soil for use as clean capping material.
- **Quantities:** Approximately **5,000 cubic yards** of native soil will be excavated from the Carpenter Road Shoofly.
- **Verification sampling outcomes:** All eight composite samples met the clean cap criteria for barium and lead.
- **Phase status:** Characterization testing is completed. Excavation and placement of the material as clean cap is **planned** to occur the week of April 19, 2021, pending DTSC approval.

### Responsible parties
- **Geocon Consultants:** Prepared the technical memorandum; performed the sampling and data analysis.
- **WSP/City of Modesto:** Recipient of the technical memorandum; construction manager for the project.
- **Caltrans:** Owner/operator of the SR 132 project and the BCS stockpiles. The soil is within the Caltrans right-of-way.
- **California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC):** Regulatory oversight agency; approved the referenced variance establishing the clean cap criteria.

### Key terms and conditions
- **Required follow-on deliverables:** The information from this memo will be included in the **Removal Action Completion Report (RACR)** to be submitted following completion of the clean fill capping activities on Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Monitoring obligations:** The material must meet the "BCS clean cap criteria" (barium ≤ 120 mg/kg) to be used as clean capping material, as established in the DTSC-approved Variance.
