Here is the structured summary for the case-file wiki.

## Document identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Technical Memorandum summarizing the results of characterization testing for soil stockpiles generated during Bent 2 excavation, to determine suitability for use as clean cap material on Stockpile 2.
- **Author or preparer:** John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG, Project Manager, Geocon.
- **Date or period covered:** October 30, 2020. Sampling event occurred on October 29, 2020; laboratory analysis completed October 30, 2020.

## Key claims
- The Bent 2 soil stockpiles do **not** meet the clean cap criteria established in the DTSC-approved Variance to Remedial Design Implementation Plan (April 9, 2020) because four of ten samples exceeded the maximum site-specific background concentration for barium (120 mg/kg).
- Pending DTSC review, the material may be **suitable for use as a partial clean cap** (≤6 inches) on the upper surface of Stockpile 2, because a structural pavement section (base material and asphalt/concrete) will be placed over the entire upper surface, providing additional cover.
- Fill from previously approved clean fill sources will be required for the **northerly slope** of Stockpile 2.

## Regulatory decisions
- No formal decision documented in this memo. The memo states the data will be provided to DTSC for review and approval consideration regarding the proposed alternative use of the soil.

## Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Excavation of approximately 4,000 cubic yards of native soil from Bent 2 of the overcrossing structure.
- **Material Management:** The soil was placed in two rows of end dumps on the southern portion of Stockpile 2. A portion of this material was placed on top of a fill berm generated from the Stockpile 2 MSE wall footing excavation, which was previously approved for use as clean cap by DTSC.
- **Phase Status:** Investigation/characterization phase.
- **Verification Outcomes:** The stockpiled material does not meet the criteria for unrestricted use as clean cap; a proposed alternative (partial cap with structural pavement) is pending DTSC approval.

## Responsible parties
- **California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC):** Regulatory agency that approved the initial variance for clean cap criteria and pending review of alternative use proposal.
- **Caltrans:** Owner/operator of the project site.
- **Central Valley RWQCB:** Co-regulator.
- **WSP/City of Modesto:** Client for the work; represented by Scott Frenette, Construction Manager.
- **Geocon Consultants:** Consultant responsible for sampling, analysis, and preparation of the technical memorandum; represented by John E. Juhrend.
- **California Laboratory Services (CLS):** Subcontracted analytical laboratory.

## Key terms and conditions
- **Pending DTSC approval:** The proposed use of the stockpiled material as a partial clean cap (6 inches or less) on the upper surface of Stockpile 2 is contingent upon DTSC review and approval.
- **Required Action:** Fill material from previously approved clean fill sources is required for placement of clean cap on the **northerly slope** of Stockpile 2.
- **Reporting Obligation:** This information will be included in the **Removal Action Completion Report** to be submitted following completion of Stockpile 1 and 2 clean fill capping activities.
