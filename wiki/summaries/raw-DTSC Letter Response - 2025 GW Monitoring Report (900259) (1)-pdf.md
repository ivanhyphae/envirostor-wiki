## Site and scope
- **Site name, location, areas of concern covered:** Caltrans Encapsulated Soil Stockpiles (also SR 132 West Expressway), south of intersection of State Route 99 and Kansas Avenue, Modesto, Stanislaus County, California. Three original stockpiles (Stockpiles 1, 2, 3) containing barium-contaminated soil from the 1960s; after Phase I, Stockpile 3 soil consolidated into Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Media addressed:** Groundwater (monitoring wells), storm water / surface water (runoff)
- **Specific monitoring wells, sample locations, or work areas referenced:** Ten groundwater monitoring wells installed in 2012; six decommissioned in 2019; the remaining four wells destroyed under the Well Destruction Completion Report. Storm water sampling at stockpile containment system (qualifying rain events). No individual well IDs or sample locations specified.
