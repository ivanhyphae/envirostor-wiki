### Document Identity
- **Document Type:** Email correspondence forwarding a proposed soil sampling plan.
- **Purpose:** To request regulatory approval from DTSC for a proposed sampling methodology to characterize soils from MSE (Mechanically Stabilized Earth) wall footings for on-site reuse.
- **Author:** Forwarded by Richard C. Stewart (Caltrans); original plan prepared by John Juhrend, P.E., C.E.G., C.E.M. (Geocon Consultants, Inc.).
- **Date:** Tuesday, March 31, 2020.

### Key Claims
- **Claim (Proposed Methodology):** Geocon asserts that as the BCS (Barium-Contaminated Soil) slope zone is removed, soil sampling will be conducted at the midpoint of the planned MSE wall footing excavation at ten locations spaced at approximately 200-foot intervals.
- **Claim (Depth & Analytics):** Discrete samples will be collected from 1-foot intervals to a depth of approximately 4–5 feet and analyzed for barium and lead under a 48-hour expedited turnaround.
- **Claim (Reuse Suitability):** The analytical data will be compared to specific regulatory screening levels to determine if the soil is suitable for reuse as clean capping material, abutment/embankment fill, or placement in BCS containment zones.
- **Attribution:** These claims are attributed to John Juhrend (Geocon) and forwarded by Richard Stewart (Caltrans).

### Regulatory Decisions
- **Decision Type:** **Pending Approval.** The email explicitly requests approval from DTSC (Dean Wright, Perry Myers) and the Central Valley RWQCB (Steven Meeks) for the proposed sampling plan.
- **Agency:** California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Condition:** Approval is required *before* proceeding with the proposed sampling of the MSE footing soils for characterization and classification.

### Analytical Results
- **Contaminants of Concern:** Barium, Lead.
- **Media:** Soil (discrete samples from 1-foot intervals).
- **Screening Levels (used for comparison):**
    - **Barium:**
        - Site-specific background (max): **120 mg/kg**
        - RDIP BCS Removal Threshold (groundwater protection): **1,000 mg/kg**
        - Construction Worker ESL: **3,000 mg/kg**
        - Residential ESL: **15,000 mg/kg**
        - Commercial/Industrial ESL: **220,000 mg/kg**
    - **Lead:**
        - Site-specific background (max): **3.8 mg/kg**
        - DTSC Residential Screening Level: **80 mg/kg**
- **Exceedances:** Noted as potential; the analysis is being conducted to determine if material meets the threshold for clean reuse vs. BCS containment.

### Remedial Actions
- **Activity:** Excavation of BCS (Barium-Contaminated Soil) slope zone on Stockpile 1; proposed soil sampling of MSE wall footings.
- **Phase:** Active Work (BCS slope zone removal began on the date of the email).
- **Verification:** R DIP (Removal Design/Implementation Plan) monitoring, including air/dust perimeter sampling, is being conducted by Dave Watts (Geocon).

### Responsible Parties

- **Regulatory Oversight:** DTSC (Dean Wright, Perry Myers, Steve Meeks).
