### Document identity
- **Document type:** Technical Memorandum
- **Preparer / author organization:** Geocon Consultants, Inc.
- **Recipient / addressee:** Rick Demi, PE, Construction Manager, WSP/City of Modesto
- **Date:** Revised July 2, 2020
- **Project number:** Geocon Project No. S1908-01-01

### Site and scope
- **Site name / location:** SR 132 Project, Modesto, CA. Proposed "Basin 5" clean cap borrow area, adjacent to and east of Carpenter Road within the SR 132 right-of-way.
- **Areas of concern covered:** Proposed borrow area to source clean cover material for capping Barium Containing Soil (BCS) Stockpiles 1 and 2.
- **Media addressed:** Soil.
- **Specific sample locations:** 6 exploratory trenches (BASIN5-1 through BASIN5-6) on a grid pattern. Samples were field-composited into 3-part composites per depth interval (0-2 ft, 2-5 ft, 5-10 ft).

### Key claims
- **Claim:** The reported metal concentrations in the composite samples are consistent with naturally occurring background concentrations.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon (John E. Juhrend, PE, CEG).
    - **Supported by data:** Yes. The claim is supported by the analytical results presented in the memo and the attached laboratory report, which are compared to site-specific background concentrations from a 2007 Shaw background data summary.
- **Claim:** The proposed Basin 5 clean cap borrow area appears suitable to generate clean cover fill materials for capping BCS Stockpiles 1 and 2.
    - **Who is making it:** Geocon.
    - **Supported by data:** Yes. This is the core conclusion of the memo, based on the data showing consistency with background for all detected analytes and non-detection for all other Title 22 metals and organochlorine pesticides (OCPs).

### Regulatory decisions
- **Decision type:** Approval (referenced).
- **Deciding agency:** California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC).
- **Date:** Not specified in this document.
- **Conditions or requirements imposed:** The sampling strategy was developed in accordance with DTSC's 2001 "Information Advisory Clean Imported Fill Soil" and was approved by Mr. Dean Wright of DTSC.
- **Documents approved or acted on:** The soil sampling strategy for the Basin 5 borrow area.

### Analytical results
- **Contaminants measured:** Title 22 metals (Antimony, Arsenic, Barium, Beryllium, Cadmium, Chromium, Cobalt, Copper, Lead, Molybdenum, Nickel, Selenium, Silver, Thallium, Vanadium, Zinc, Mercury) and Organochlorine Pesticides (OCPs).
- **Media:** Soil.
- **Concentration ranges (mg/kg):**
    - Arsenic: <1.0 to 1.8 (background: 1.2)
    - Barium: 66 to 94 (background: 72.8)
    - Chromium: 8.5 to 11 (background: 8.6)
    - Cobalt: 3.3 to 3.8 (background: 4.4)
    - Copper: 7.5 to 9.3 (background: 7.5)
    - Lead: 5.8 to 11 (background: 2.0)
    - Nickel: 6.2 to 7.9 (background: 5.3)
    - Vanadium: 33 to 37 (background: 31.3)
    - Zinc: 29 to 36 (background: 26.3)
- **Applicable thresholds:** Site-specific background concentrations from a 2007 Shaw background data summary.
- **Locations exceeding thresholds:** No locations exceeded background concentrations based on the data presented.
- **Non-detects:** Antimony, Beryllium, Cadmium, Molybdenum, Selenium, Silver, Thallium, Mercury, and all OCP analytes were not detected above laboratory reporting limits.
- **QA/QC notes:** The attached laboratory report by Advanced Technology Laboratories (ATL) is included. A single Matrix Spike recovery outside of acceptance limits was noted for Selenium (flag M1), but the batch was validated by the Laboratory Control Sample.

### Remedial actions
- **Activity:** Soil characterization and sampling of a proposed borrow area (Basin 5).
- **Phase status:** Completed (sampling and analysis phase). The information will be included in a future "Removal Action Completion Report" to be submitted after stockpile capping is finished.
