## Document identity
- **Document type and purpose:** Approval letter from the Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) to the California Department of Transportation (Caltrans), concurring with the revised Supplemental Site Investigation (SSI) and Human Health Risk Assessment (HHRA) Update for the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles project.
- **Author or preparer:** Randy S. Adams, C.E.G., Senior Engineering Geologist, Brownfields and Environmental Restoration Program, DTSC.
- **Date or period covered:** Letter dated April 4, 2013. Reports under review are dated March 1, 2013; work was conducted following a September 2012 workplan.

## Key claims
- DTSC, in consultation with the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (RWQCB), has reviewed and concurs with the revised SSI and HHRA Update reports (both dated March 1, 2013, prepared by Geocon Consultants Inc.). *Attribution: DTSC.*
- The reports adequately respond to DTSC's prior comments (February 15, 2013) and address the purpose of the approved Final Supplemental Site Characterization Workplan (September 18, 2012), which was to evaluate lateral and vertical migration of contaminants from soil stockpiles and update the HHRA. *Attribution: DTSC.*

## Regulatory decisions
- **Decision:** Approval of the revised SSI report and HHRA Update. The reports may be used to proceed with preparing a Remedial Action Plan.
- **Agency:** DTSC (in consultation with RWQCB).
- **Date:** April 4, 2013.
- **Conditions implied:** Reports are accepted as submitted; DTSC oversight continues under an Interagency Agreement and Task Order with Caltrans.

## Analytical results
- No specific concentration ranges, thresholds, or exceedances are presented in this letter. The document references the underlying reports for characterization data on barium-contaminated fill material.

## Remedial actions
- No physical remedial work is described in this letter. The document covers characterization and risk assessment phases only.

## Responsible parties
- **California Department of Transportation (Caltrans):** Owner/operator; responsible for implementing the workplan and reports under DTSC oversight.
- **Geocon Consultants Inc. (Geocon):** Contractor preparing the reports.
- **DTSC:** Lead regulatory oversight agency (Brownfields and Environmental Restoration Program).
- **Central Valley RWQCB:** Co-regulatory agency, consulted on approval.
- **Stanislaus County Health Agency:** Notified as interested party.

## Key terms and conditions
- The approved SSI and HHRA Update will support preparation of a Remedial Action Plan for management of the soil stockpiles.
- Oversight is conducted under an Interagency Agreement and Task Order between Caltrans and DTSC.
- No specific deadlines, monitoring obligations, or land-use restrictions are stated in this letter.
