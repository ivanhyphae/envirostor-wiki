## Overview

A Feasibility Study (FS) is the site‑specific analysis that identifies, evaluates, and recommends remedial alternatives for contaminated media. In the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles case (SR 132, Stanislaus County), the FS serves as the bridge between the Remedial Investigation (RI) and the selection of a final remedy. It documents the technical, regulatory, and cost considerations that support the agency’s decision, typically following DTSC’s guidance under California’s Hazardous Waste Control Law.
