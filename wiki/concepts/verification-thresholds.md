## Verification Thresholds

### Overview
Verification thresholds are the contaminant concentration limits used to determine whether excavation of barium‑contaminated soil (BCS) at the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles site (SR 132, Stanislaus County) has been successful. For Stockpile 3, two thresholds were applied: **1,000 mg/kg for barium** (groundwater protection) and **80 mg/kg for lead** (residential screening level). These values were specified in the Removal Design Implementation Plan (RDIP) and were used to evaluate post‑excavation verification soil samples collected from the base and berms of the excavation.

### Site Record
- **Document source:** *SR 132 Stockpile 3 BCS Removal Tech Memo* (undated, likely 2020).
- **Thresholds stated:**
  - Barium: 1,000 mg/kg (groundwater protection).
  - Lead: 80 mg/kg (residential screening level).
- **Excavation and sampling:**
  - Stockpile 3 was excavated to elevations between 82.5 and 84 ft above mean sea level (original ground surface at 84 ft).
  - A minimum of 24 verification samples were required; the memo shows 24 sample locations (S3 1‑0 through S3 24‑0), plus two berm samples (S3 SE BERM, S3 SW BERM).
  - Samples were collected between February 28 and March 12, 2020 using stainless steel tubes, analyzed by ATL via EPA Method 6010B for barium and lead.
- **Exceedance and corrective action:**
  - Initial results for S3 9‑0 through S3 11‑0 showed barium > 1,000 mg/kg. These areas were deepened ~1 ft and resampled.
- **Final results:**
  - All verification samples (including resamples) contained barium between 46 and 970 mg/kg and lead between 1.6 and 40 mg/kg.
  - The memo states that “each of the final excavation soil samples contained barium and lead below the RDIP verification thresholds.”
- **Outcome:** Stockpile 3 BCS removal was deemed complete and suitable for placement of clean embankment fill without further BCS RDIP monitoring.

### Regulatory Status
- The verification thresholds were applied under the RDIP; no agency citation (e.g., DTSC, RWQCB) is provided in the memo, but the thresholds correspond to common California screening levels for residential soil (lead) and groundwater protection (barium).
- The project is in compliance with the RDIP thresholds for Stockpile 3. The information will be included in a future Removal Action Completion Report (RACR) after Stockpile 1 and 2 capping.

### Open Items
- The *Removal Action Completion Report* for the entire site has not yet been submitted (pending completion of Stockpile 1 and 2 clean fill capping activities). Data from Stockpile 3 will be incorporated into that report.
