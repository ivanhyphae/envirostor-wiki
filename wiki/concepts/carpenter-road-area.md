## Overview
The Carpenter Road Area is a geographic designation within the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation case file (SR 132, Stanislaus County). It identifies the location of the main stockpile staging, pre‑processing, and loadout operations associated with the highway widening project. The area served as the physical nexus for soil receiving, analytical characterization, treatment (amendment, mixing, stockpile management), and eventual off‑site disposal or beneficial reuse.
