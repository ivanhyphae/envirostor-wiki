## Overview

Caltrans State Route 132 Modesto Soil Stockpiles is a remediation and operations and maintenance (O&M) site located in Stanislaus County, California. The site involves two soil stockpiles under containment zones (Stockpile 1 and Stockpile 2) on State Route 132. The project is managed by Caltrans and subject to regulatory oversight by the California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC). The site is in an active O&M phase, with annual inspections conducted to monitor containment integrity and surface conditions. The O&M Site Inspection Form, dated March 18, 2025, documents the inspection performed by Adam Inman and represents the most recent available project record.
