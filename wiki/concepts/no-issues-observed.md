## Overview

“No Issues Observed” is a notation entered by the inspector on the **Annual Stockpile Inspection Form** for the Caltrans SR 132 Modesto Soil Stockpiles O&M Site. It appears in the “Observations, Recommended Corrective Action, and Summary of Corrective Action Repairs” column for the **Stockpile 1 Containment Zone** under the “Other” row, indicating that during the inspection cycle no actionable deficiencies were identified beyond the standard check items already assessed. This finding is documented for the inspection conducted on **March 18, 2025**, by inspector **Adam Inman**.

On the same form, all standard check items for Stockpile 1—including MSE walls (bulging/out-of-plane), highway pavement (cracks/potholes), median and shoulders (cracks/potholes), north slope (vegetation/rills/slumping), and storm drains (debris/obstructions/erosion)—were checked “No” for needing repairs/maintenance and “No” for surface condition problems, consistent with the “No issues observed” summary. The inspection form for **Stockpile 2 Containment Zone** was partially completed in the source record; the available data show the MSE wall, highway pavement, median/shoulders, and north slope items were similarly checked “No” for issues and repairs.
