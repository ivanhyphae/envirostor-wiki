## Overview

**Barium Containing Soil (BCS)** is the project-specific designation for soil at the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles (SR 132, Stanislaus County) that was impacted by barium at concentrations exceeding the cleanup thresholds established in the Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP). BCS constituted a discrete waste material requiring excavation and off-site disposal or treatment, and its removal was a key task in the multi-stockpile remediation program.

## Site Record

- **Stockpile 2 – BCS Removal**  
  A Technical Memorandum dated March 9, 2020 documented the removal of Barium Containing Soil from the east and west ends of Stockpile 2. Confirmation soil sampling performed after removal verified that those areas met the RDIP verification thresholds. On March 10, 2020, DTSC Project Manager Dean Wright e-mailed Caltrans approving the memo and confirming that the east and west ends of Stockpile 2 were suitable for placement of clean embankment fill.  
  *Source:* "Stockpile 2 Confirmation Sampling Tech Memo Approval.pdf" – email chain from Richard Stewart (Caltrans) to Dean Wright (DTSC), March 9–10, 2020.

- **Stockpile 3 – BCS Removal**  
  A separate Technical Memorandum (filename "SR 132 Stockpile 3 BCS Removal Tech Memo") covered BCS removal at Stockpile 3. As of March 9, 2020, removal operations were expected to be completed the following day, pending final verification testing results. No subsequent acceptance or rejection from DTSC is documented in the provided sources.  
  *Source:* "SR 132 Stockpile 3 BCS Removal Tech Memo.md" – email from John Juhrend (Geocon) to Richard Stewart, March 9, 2020.

## Regulatory Status

- Applicable standards for BCS removal are defined in the RDIP. The DTSC acceptance for Stockpile 2 confirms that the areas achieved those verification thresholds.  
- The Stockpile 2 areas are now classed as suitable for clean fill placement, with no further remedial action required for barium at those locations (as of March 2020).  
- The regulatory status of Stockpile 3 is pending completion of final verification testing; no approval or rejection is recorded in the supplied documents.
