## Overview

Total Petroleum Hydrocarbons (TPH) are a broad class of chemical compounds derived from crude oil, used in this project as the primary contaminant of concern for soil stockpiles associated with State Route 132 improvements in Stanislaus County, California. At the Modesto Soil Stockpiles site, TPH contamination originates from historical petroleum releases, likely related to prior industrial or transportation uses. TPH is the target analyte for remediation under the approved Soil Management Plan (SMP), as confirmed by the California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) in the Final SMP Approval E-mail.
