## Overview

The **Bent 2 Soils Tech Memo** is a technical memorandum dated October 30, 2020, submitted by Caltrans (Richard C. Stewart) to DTSC and the Water Board to characterize excavated soils from the Bent 2 column at the SR 132 site in Stanislaus County, California. The memo evaluated whether these excavation soils met the clean cap criteria established in the April 9, 2020 Variance Request approved by DTSC, for potential reuse as clean cap material on Stockpile 2. The analytical results showed that the soils exceeded the background criteria for clean cap cover material, meaning the soils did not meet the originally approved reuse standard.

## Site Record

- **Document Date:** October 30, 2020
- **Submitted By:** Richard C. Stewart, Engineering Geologist, Caltrans Central Region Environmental Division
- **Submission Date to Regulators:** November 2, 2020 (via email)
- **Regulatory Acceptance:** November 6, 2020, via email from Dean Wright (DTSC Project Manager)
- **Analytical Results:** The soil sample results from the Bent 2 column excavation exceeded the background criteria established in the April 9, 2020 Variance Request for use as clean cap cover material.
- **Regulatory Decision:** Both DTSC and the Water Board agreed that the soil could be placed on top of Stockpile 2 at a thickness of 6 inches or less, provided it is covered with clean base material and the asphalt/concrete roadway. This accommodated the project schedule while not jeopardizing remedy integrity.
- **Temporal Context:** Due to the project schedule, Bent 2 soils might be temporarily exposed to the elements during the winter months (late 2020/early 2021).
- **Associated Documents:** The acceptance email also approved the Revised Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP) Amendment dated October 22, 2020.

## Regulatory Status

- **Applicable Standard:** The clean cap criteria were set in the April 9, 2020 Variance Request approved by DTSC. The Bent 2 soils did not meet these background-based criteria.
- **Agencies Involved:** DTSC (Dean Wright, Project Manager) and the Water Board (Steven Meeks) jointly reviewed and accepted the memo.
- **Conditions of Acceptance:** The soil may be used under the following conditions:
  - Placement thickness: 6 inches or less on top of Stockpile 2.
  - Must be covered with clean base material and roadway asphalt/concrete.
  - Rainy season surface water sampling and erosion control measures from the Revised SWPPP Amendment (October 22, 2020) must be strictly followed.
- **Status:** Approved with conditions; project in compliance subject to those conditions.

## Open Items

- **Winter Exposure:** Because the Bent 2 soils may remain temporarily exposed during winter months, strict adherence to the Revised SWPPP Amendment’s surface water sampling and erosion control measures is required to prevent off-site migration of contaminants.
