## Overview

**BG1** is a designated background surface water sampling location within the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation project (SR 132, Stanislaus County, California). It is one of two original background locations (BG1 and BG2) established by Geocon between April 2013 and April 2019, and continued by Stantec under Contract 06A2542, Task Order 97. BG1 serves as a reference point to characterize ambient (non-impacted) surface water quality against which runoff from the soil stockpiles is compared.

## Site Record

- **Establishment:** BG1 was first installed as part of the surface water sampling network under the SAP Addendum, approved by DTSC and the Central Valley Regional Water Quality Control Board (CVRWQCB). The location was used by Geocon for on-call sampling between April 2013 and April 2019.
- **Continued monitoring:** Under On-Call Services Contract 06A2542, Stantec performed surface water sampling at BG1 during the 2019–2020 period.
- **February 28, 2023 inspection:** During a site walk on November 2, 2022, Caltrans and Stantec confirmed that BG1 remained in the sampling network (while BG2 was removed and BG3 was added). On February 28, 2023, Stantec mobilized for a surface water inspection following a rain event (0.09 inches recorded that day). The photographic record (Appendix A, Photograph 1) shows BG1 with **no visible water ponding** around the drain at the time of inspection.
- **Physical setting:** The source document notes that "the area around the background surface water location BG1 has not changed" as of the February 2023 walk.

## Regulatory Status

- **Approval agencies:** The sample locations—including BG1—were approved by DTSC and CVRWQCB.
- **Applicability:** No specific background thresholds or action levels for BG1 are documented in the provided source material. However, as a background station, it functions as a reference point for determining whether runoff from the stockpiles exceeds naturally occurring concentrations.
- **Current compliance:** No exceedances or non-compliance issues related to BG1 are reported in the source documents.

## Open Items

- None documented regarding BG1. The location remains in the active monitoring network as of the February 2023 inspection.
