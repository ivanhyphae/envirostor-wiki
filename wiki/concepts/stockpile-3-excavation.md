## Overview

The **Stockpile 3 Excavation** was a remedial action conducted under the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles project (SR 132, Stanislaus County, California). The excavation targeted contaminated bulk soil stored in Stockpile 3, a designated soil stockpile on the project site. The removal was documented in the *SR 132 Stockpile 3 BCS Removal Tech Memo*, and subsequent confirmation sampling was approved by regulatory authorities via the *Stockpile 3 Confirmation Sampling Tech Memo Approval*. The excavation was part of a larger Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP) that addressed multiple stockpiles across the site.
