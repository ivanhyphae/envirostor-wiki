## Overview

BG3 is a designated background surface water sampling location established for the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation project (SR 132, Stanislaus County, CA). It was added to the surface water monitoring network on November 2, 2022, during a joint site walk with Caltrans and Stantec, replacing a previously removed background sample (BG2). Its purpose is to establish ambient (non-impacted) surface water quality conditions for comparison with project-area runoff during rain events.

## Site Record

- **Location:** Approximately 700 feet south of the southeast corner of North Carpenter Road and Kansas Avenue, along the east side of North Carpenter Road (per Figure 2 of the referenced Surface Water Letter).
- **Establishment:** Added on November 2, 2022, during a site walk with Caltrans and Stantec. Background sample BG2 was removed from the network at that time.
- **Inspection Date:** February 28, 2023 (mobilized at ~8:45 am).
- **Precipitation Measured:** 0.09 inches on February 28, 2023 (overnight rain; minimal rain during inspection).
- **Observations (February 28, 2023 inspection):**
  - No visible water ponding was observed in the area around BG3.
  - No active runoff or surface water discharge was observed at any of the project-area surface water locations (including BG3, PL6, PL7, and BG1).
- **Documentation:**
  - Surface Water Letter (final, March 9, 2023) – Letter to Mr. Richard Stewart, PG, from Stantec.
  - Appendix A Photographic Record (photograph ID 2) – Image of BG3 showing no water present around the drain.
  - The inspection report notes no discharge/runoff throughout the project area.

## Regulatory Status

- **Applicable Standards:** The project uses background surface water locations (including BG3) to establish baseline conditions against which potential exceedances of California Surface Water Quality Objectives (e.g., for metals, PCBs, or other constituents) are compared. No specific action levels (e.g., MCLs) are explicitly stated in the source for BG3 itself.
- **Compliance:** The February 28, 2023 inspection found no active runoff or water ponding at BG3, consistent with the observation that no project-related discharges were occurring during the sampled event.

## Open Items

- The inspection report notes that hydroseed lacks vegetation in some areas of Stockpile 2 and minor erosion at Stockpile 1 slopes, but these observations are unrelated to BG3.
- No further sampling or analysis of BG3 is documented in the surface water letter; the site is intended as a background location for future rain-event sampling.
