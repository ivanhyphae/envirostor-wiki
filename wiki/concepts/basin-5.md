## Overview

**Basin 5** is a surface impoundment or stormwater management basin located within the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation site along State Route 132 (SR 132) in Stanislaus County, California. The basin receives and contains stormwater runoff from the soil stockpile areas undergoing remediation. It is a key monitoring point for assessing whether the stockpiles are releasing contaminants of concern (primarily arsenic and barium) into stormwater that could migrate offsite. The basin is subject to routine stormwater sampling under the project's Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP) and associated Water Discharge Requirements.
