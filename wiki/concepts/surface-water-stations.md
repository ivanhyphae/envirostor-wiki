## Surface Water Stations

### Overview
Surface water stations are discrete monitoring locations established to assess water quality in drainage features and receiving waters adjacent to the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles along State Route 132 in Stanislaus County. Their role in the remediation case file is to provide data on potential contaminant migration from the stockpiles via surface runoff, and to support compliance with discharge and water quality standards. The stations are documented in the March 2018 surface water sampling event, the primary source for this article.
