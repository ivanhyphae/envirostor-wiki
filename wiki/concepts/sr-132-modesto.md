## Overview

The SR 132 Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation case file documents the cleanup of contaminated soil stockpiles associated with the State Route 132 (SR 132) Modesto Bypass project in Stanislaus County, California. The case file centers on the regulatory approval of a Soil Management Plan (SMP) by the California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC), which established the framework for characterization, handling, and off-site disposal of impacted soil generated during construction. The project is under the purview of Caltrans (California Department of Transportation).
