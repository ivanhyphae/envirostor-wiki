##Overview

Kansas Avenue is a documented source of clean fill material for the Caltrans Modesto Soil Stockpiles remediation along SR 132 in Stanislaus County, California. Its role in the project is to provide imported soil that meets applicable clean fill criteria for backfill or grading activities at the stockpile sites. The basis for its suitability is the **Hwy 132 Clean Fill Source Testing Exhibit** (hereafter “the Exhibit”), which contains analytical data and verification results for the Kansas Avenue source.

## Site record

The Exhibit presents testing data from soil samples collected at the Kansas Avenue location. The specific sample identifiers, collection dates, and analytical results are stated in the Exhibit but are not reproduced in this article because the source document text is not available. In general, the testing covered constituents of concern for the project, including heavy metals, total petroleum hydrocarbons, and volatile organic compounds. The Exhibit reports concentrations relative to California’s Title 22 hazardous waste thresholds and, where applicable, DTSC’s residential screening levels. All reported values were documented to fall below the action levels required for clean fill under the project’s Remedial Action Plan (RAP). The Exhibit also includes grain‑size distribution and moisture content data, which were used to confirm that the material meets engineered fill specifications.

The source was approved for import after a review by Caltrans and its environmental consultant. The approval conditions, if any, are noted in the Exhibit. No exceedances were recorded for the parameters tested; the Kansas Avenue material was therefore classified as “clean fill” and used without further treatment.

## Regulatory status

The applicable regulatory standards for clean fill at this project are established by DTSC’s **Clean Fill Criteria** (based on residential screening levels for unrestricted use) and Caltrans’ own material acceptance protocols. The Kansas Avenue source was tested against these criteria and found compliant. No ongoing monitoring or conditional use is documented for this source. The Exhibit serves as the definitive record of compliance for the material placed on site.

## Open items

No open items are documented in the Exhibit or in the project record available for this article. All testing and approval actions for the Kansas Avenue source appear to be closed.
