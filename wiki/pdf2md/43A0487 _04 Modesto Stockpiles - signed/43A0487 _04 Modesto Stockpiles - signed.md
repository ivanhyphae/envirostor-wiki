## I. Service Request Purpose

This Service Request between the California Department of Transportation (Caltrans) and the California Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) is to reimburse costs incurred by DTSC for regulatory oversight of the environmental work associated with the Modesto Soil Stockpiles (Site) to support construction of Phase 2 of the State Route 132 West Expressway.

## II. Scope of Services

DTSC will provide professional staff services and conduct all activities, as needed, in providing regulatory oversight for Caltrans activities associated with the project identified above.

The DTSC specific tasks include, but are not limited to, the following:

- A. Review and comment on routine monitoring and/or inspection reports.
- B. Review and comment on Remedial Design Implementation Plans and associated documents.
- C. Review and comment on well destruction reports

- E. Provide field oversight, as needed by DTSC, of monitoring and field activities conducted by Caltrans.

## III. Period of Performance

Work under this Service Request shall begin August 4, 2025, or upon execution and terminate on June 30, 2030.

## IV. Schedule

Work items will be implemented as needed and shall progress in such a manner that the work is complete and submitted for approval as soon as practical. The deliverable and review schedule for any work component will be negotiated by the Caltrans and DTSC Service Request Managers. A minimum of 30-day review period is assumed for DTSC review of submitted documents. DTSC shall exercise due diligence to ensure that all activities are conducted in a timely manner.

## V. Reports

There will be no formal progress reports, or other reports, required under this Service Request. Caltrans may request a copy of the daily logs and timesheets of staff who are listed in the quarterly invoice from DTSC.

- A. DTSC will provide written comments and recommendations based on submittal of data, reports, and other information.
- B. Each billing invoice submitted by DTSC shall include summary of activities of indirect and direct charges and daily logs provided by DTSC to Caltrans for the invoice period.

## VI. Meetings

The DTSC Service Request Manager shall meet with the Caltrans Service Request Manager, Contract Manager, staff, and contractors as necessary to discuss project progress, or upon request from Caltrans. Caltrans may request monthly meetings or conference calls with DTSC to discuss project progress. Informal meetings may be held at either party's request to discuss project issues or problems encountered. At a minimum, DTSC will meet or confer with Caltrans following review of a report.
