In accordance with Health and Safety Code Section 25269.5, the Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) is providing Responsible Parties/Project Proponents with an estimate of DTSC's oversight costs for project activities scheduled for the period from July 1, 2022 through June 30, 2023.

The tasks scheduled to be conducted during this period for this project are:

- 2. Fiscal Year Cost Estimate;
- 3. Review and approval Interim Phase 1 Remedial Action Completion Report (RACR);
- 4. Complete the (O&M) Agreement;
