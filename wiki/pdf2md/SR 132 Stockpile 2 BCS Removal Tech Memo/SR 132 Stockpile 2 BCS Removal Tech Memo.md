Subject: Stockpile 2 BCS Removal Verification Testing

State Route 132 Project, Modesto, California

This Technical Memorandum presents a summary of barium containing soil (BCS) removal verification testing completed at the west and east ends of Stockpile 2 within the State Route 132 right-of-way (see attached plan sheets). The BCS excavation areas include the west end of Stockpile 2 adjacent to Emerald Avenue and the east end of Stockpile 2 adjacent to State Route 99. BCS was removed from these areas to facilitate construction of bridge overcrossing structures. The removed BCS was placed as embankment fill within the designated Stockpile 2 fill containment zones.

The BCS removal verification testing was performed in general accordance with the January 2019 Remedial Design Implementation Plan (RDIP) prepared for the State Route 132 Project. The RDIP provides the following verification testing thresholds, excavation elevations and minimum number of verification soil samples:

#### **BCS Removal Verification Testing Removal Thresholds**

Barium - 1,000 milligrams per kilogram (mg/kg, groundwater protection) Lead - 80 mg/kg (residential screening level)

| Excavation Area      | Original Ground Surface Elevation (Above Mean Sea Level [MSL]) | Minimum Number of Verification Samples |
|----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| West End Stockpile 2 | 80                                                             | 8                                      |
| East End Stockpile 2 | 82                                                             | 12                                     |

The west end of Stockpile 2 was excavated to an approximate elevation of 82 feet above MSL. Eight soil samples (S2W 1-0 through 8-0) were collected on February 28, 2020 from the base of the excavation. Following receipt of the initial analytical data indicating elevated barium concentrations above the verification threshold for sample locations S2W 1-0 and S2W 3-0, the western half of the excavation was deepened to an approximate elevation of 81 feet above MSL. Two additional verification soil samples were collected from the deepened excavation on March 6, 2020.

The eastern half of the east end of Stockpile 2 was excavated to an approximate elevation of 83 feet above MSL. Six soil samples (S2E 1-0 through 8-0) were collected on February 21, 2020 from the

![](_page_0_Picture_15.jpeg)

base of the excavation. Following receipt of the initial analytical data indicating an elevated barium concentration above the verification threshold for sample location S2E 3-0, the area around this sample location and the western half of the excavation was excavated to an approximate elevation of 82 feet above MSL. Seven additional verification soil samples (S2E 3A-0 and S2E 7-0 through 12-0) were collected from the western half of the excavation on February 28, 2020. Only sample location SE2 8-0 contained an elevated barium concentration above the verification threshold. This area was subsequently excavated to an approximate elevation of 81 feet above MSL. One additional verification soil sample was collected from the deepened excavation on March 6, 2020.

The BCS removal verification soil samples were collected from the base of the removal areas that exposed native soil conditions using new stainless steel sample tubes that were transported to Advanced Technology Laboratories (ATL) under chain of custody protocol. Each soil sample was analyzed for barium and lead following Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Test Method 6010B. The approximate verification soil sample locations are depicted on the attached plan sheets. A summary of the verification soil sample analytical data is on Table 1. Copies of the ATL laboratory reports are attached.

Each of the final excavation soil samples contained barium and lead below the RDIP verification thresholds. Barium concentrations ranged from 54 to 760 mg/kg, and lead concentrations from 1.5 to 35 mg/kg. Based on the data presented herein, the BCS removal at the west and east ends of Stockpile 2 has met the barium and lead verification thresholds and is suitable for placement of clean embankment fill. This information will be included in the Removal Action Completion Report to be submitted following completion of the stockpile clean fill capping activities.
