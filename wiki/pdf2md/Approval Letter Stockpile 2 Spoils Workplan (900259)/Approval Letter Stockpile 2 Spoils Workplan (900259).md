## Dear Mr. Juhrend:

The Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC) has reviewed the Technical Memorandum for Stockpile 2 Barium Containing Soil (BCS) Foundation Spoil Assessment Workplan (Tech Memo) dated February 28, 2022.

The Tech Memo was submitted by Geocon Consultants Inc. on behalf of the Department of Transportation (Caltrans) to describe the planned sampling and management of BCS spoils generated from the installation of the Weather Station and Overhead Sign #1 on Stockpile 2 at the State Route 132 West project.

DTSC concurs with the stockpiling and sampling procedures described in the Tech Memo. DTSC also agrees that if the sampling results indicate concentrations of barium and lead are below the established site-specific background, the material may be reused on-site at locations mutually agreed upon by both DTSC and Caltrans. In addition, DTSC agrees that if sampling results show the spoils material is above background concentrations, the material should be profiled for disposal at the Forward Landfill in Manteca or another acceptable landfill facility.
