## Background Information

Under an Interagency Agreement and Task Order with Caltrans, DTSC in consultation with RWQCB is providing oversight for the purpose of preparing a Remedial Action Plan for the management of the soil stockpiles including additional characterization and updating of the HHRA. In correspondence dated September 20, 2012, DTSC reviewed and approved the "Final Supplemental Site Characterization Workplan, Modesto Soil Stockpiles, State Route 132/99, Stanislaus County, California" (Geocon, September 18, 2012). The Final Workplan provides additional characterization of soil stockpiles at the location of the proposed State Route 132/99 West Expressway to evaluate and finalize a remedy for the management of hazardous substances in the soil stockpiles. The additional characterization data is to evaluate potential lateral and vertical migration of contaminants from the soil stockpiles and to update the HHRA.
